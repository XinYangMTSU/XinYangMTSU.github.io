🎓 Word Study: Crouch Down, Ruffled, Tipped, & Roly-Poly Bugs

🧍‍♂️ "Crouch Down" 的表达

English Meaning Example 中文翻译
🧎 Kneel down Go on knees She kneeled to tie her shoes. 跪下来
🧍‍♀️ Squat down Lower body with bent knees He squatted to pick up the toy. 蹲下
🙇 Bend down Lean forward She bent down to look. 弯下身

🐱 "Ruffled" 的表达

English Meaning Example 中文翻译
Stroked Gently moved hand I stroked the cat’s back. 抚摸
Petted Touched with affection She petted the puppy. 爱抚
Tousled Made messy softly He tousled his son's hair. 轻轻揉乱

🪨 "Tipped" 的意思

Definition: Gently tilted or pushed something so it changed position.

Example: I tipped the rock over on its side.

中文解释： 轻轻把某物倾斜或翻倒（比如把石头翻过来）。

🐞 "Roly-Poly Bugs" 是什么？

Definition: A cute or childlike name for pill bugs or woodlice that roll into balls when disturbed.

Example: The rock was hiding a billion roly-poly bugs!

中文解释： “roly-poly bugs” 指的是潮虫，会卷成小球，孩子们常这样叫它们。

✏️ Sentence Comparison

Original: "What is it, Sassafras?" I crouched down and ruffled my cat’s fluffy fur.
中文翻译： “怎么啦，Sassafras？”我蹲下来，轻轻揉了揉我那只毛茸茸猫咪的毛。

Rewrite 1: I bent down and stroked my cat’s soft coat.

Rewrite 2: I squatted beside her and ran my fingers through her fur.

Rewrite 3: I knelt and gently tousled her fluffy hair.
