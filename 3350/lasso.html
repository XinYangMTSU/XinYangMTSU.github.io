High-Dimensional Breast Cancer Data (Polynomial Expansion, degree = 3)
scikit-learn PolynomialFeatures Regularization Coefficient Shrinkage

Overview

We compare three linear models on a high-dimensional expansion of the Breast Cancer dataset: OLS (no regularization), Ridge (ℓ2), and Lasso (ℓ1). After a cubic polynomial expansion, the feature space explodes, making OLS overfit while regularized methods maintain generalization.
