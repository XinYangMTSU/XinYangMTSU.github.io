Late Submission Policy

  • ★ Generally each OLA assignment must be turned in to the D2L repository by the end of the due date.
  • ★ You may turn in the OLA late by 2 days without penalty If you have reasonable reasons, e.g. you are in an extreme situation (health issue, family emergencies, and so on). (You must discuss your eligibility with the instructor before the due date.)
  • ★ Late Submission Policy:
    • 1 day late (-10 points)
    • 2 days late (-20 points)
    • 3 days late (-30 points)
    • 4 days late (-40 points)
    • 5 days late (-50 points)
    • 6 days late (-70 points)
    • 7 days late (-90 points)
  • ★ Overdue assignments will not be accepted 7 days after the due date.
  • ★ No Make Up for the Final Project Presentation

Help

  • ★ Feel free to ask the tutor questions if you need help on an open lab assignment.

OLA Grading

  • ★ Open lab assignments are graded on a 100-point scale.
  • ★ Graded assignments are returned the next week.
  • ★ The maximum grade you could get is 60/100 if your source code has syntax errors.
  • ★ It is a good idea to save graded assignments; if there is any disagreement about the recorded grades, you will need to show the graded assignment to document the mistake.
