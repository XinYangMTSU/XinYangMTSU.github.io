Guidelines & Requirements

Project Requirements

Your final project must include all the following:
  • ★ Dataset Selection: Any dataset not used in class (Kaggle, UCI, Google Dataset Search, etc.)
  • ★ Data Preprocessing & EDA: Cleaning, handling missing data, visualization (matplotlib)
  • ★ Algorithms: Use and compare at least 3 ML models
  • ★ Cross-Validation: Use 5-fold or 10-fold CV for all models
  • ★ Evaluation Metrics:
    • ☆ Classification: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1, Confusion Matrix, ROC/AUC
    • ☆ Regression: MSE, RMSE, R^2
    • ☆ Comparison table for all models
  • ★ Code: Python with scikit-learn, pandas, numpy, matplotlib, etc.
  • ★ Visualizations: Include plots (plots, histogram, ROC, etc.)
  • ★ Final Report (PDF): 5-10 pages
    (Please write your report using LaTeX in Overleaf)
    • ☆ Introduction
    • ☆ Methodology
    • ☆ Experiments & Results
    • ☆ Discussion & Conclusion
  • ★ Presentation: 15 minutes per group
  • ★ Group Size: At most two people per group

Algorithms

You may choose from (but are not limited to) the following algorithms:

Classification: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, SVM, KNN, Naive Bayes, Random Forest, Neural Network Classifier

Regression: Linear Regression, Polynomial Regression, Ridge, Lasso, Elastic Net, SVR, Random Forest Regressor, Neural Network Regressor
