Lesson 7 — New Regularized GLM

Add cortical smoothness to the first-level GLM objective.

1. Add one new term to ordinary GLM

Ordinary GLM only fits the data. The new method fits the data and also rewards cortical smoothness.

\[ \hat B=\arg\min_B \frac12\|Y-XB\|_F^2+ \frac{\lambda}{2}\operatorname{tr}(BLB^\top) \]

2. First term: fit the data

\[\frac12\|Y-XB\|_F^2\]

This says: predicted BOLD \(XB\) should be close to measured BOLD \(Y\).

3. Second term: smooth along cortex

\[\frac{\lambda}{2}\operatorname{tr}(BLB^\top)\]

This says: coefficient maps should not jump wildly between neighboring surface vertices.
