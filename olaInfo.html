Open Lab Informataion

OLA Policy

  • ★ Generally each open lab assignment must be turned in to the ranger repository by the end of the due date.
  • ★ You may turn in the OLA late by 2 days without penalty if you have reasonable reasonse, e.g. you are in an extrem situation (health issue, family emergecies, and so on). (You need to discuss with the instructor about your egilibility before the due date.)
  • ★ Late Submission Policy:
    • 1 day late (-10 points)
    • 2 day late (-20 points)
    • 3 day late (-30 points)
    • 4 day late (-40 points)
    • 5 day late (-50 points)
    • 6 day late (-70 points)
    • 7 day late (-90 points)
  • ★ Overdue open lab assignments will not be accepted 7 days after the due date.

Help

  • ★ Feel free to ask the tutor questions if you need help on a open lab assignment.

OLA Grading

  • ★ Open lab assignments are graded on a 100 points scale.
  • ★ Graded assignments are returned the next week.
  • ★ The maximum grade you could get is 60/100 if your source code has syntax errors.
  • ★ It is a good idea to save graded assignments; if there is any disagreement about the recorded grades, you will need to show the graded assignment to document the mistake.
