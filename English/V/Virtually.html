🌐 virtually /ˈvɜːrtʃuəli/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

Virtually means almost or nearly, but not completely; in some cases, it also refers to things done through computers or online systems.
virtually 意为“几乎、差不多”，有时也指通过计算机或网络系统进行的虚拟操作。

Example: Virtually all the students passed the exam.
例句：几乎所有学生都通过了考试。
Example: The meeting was held virtually due to travel restrictions.
例句：由于旅行限制，会议是在线举行的。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Nearly 几乎
  • Almost 差不多
  • Practically 实际上
  • Effectively 有效地
  • Remotely 远程地

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Virtually" comes from the Latin word virtus meaning "power" or "essence." Over time, it came to mean "in effect or essence, if not in fact."
virtually 源自拉丁语 virtus，意为“力量”或“本质”，后来引申为“实际上”或“本质上”但并非事实。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: virtus – strength, essence
拉丁语 virtus：力量，本质
⬇️
Late Latin / Old French: virtualis – effective in essence
晚期拉丁语 / 古法语 virtualis：实质上的，有效的
⬇️
Modern English: virtually – nearly or by computer
现代英语 virtually：几乎，或通过计算机方式
