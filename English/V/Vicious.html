😈 vicious /ˈvɪʃəs/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Vicious means deliberately cruel or violent. It can also describe something very harmful, intense, or dangerous.
vicious 的意思是“恶毒的”或“凶残的”，也可以用来形容某事物非常严重、危险或有害。

Example: The dog gave a vicious growl at the stranger.
例句：那只狗对陌生人发出恶狠狠的低吼。
Example: She launched a vicious attack against his character.
例句：她对他的人格进行了恶毒的攻击。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Cruel 残忍的
  • Brutal 残暴的
  • Malicious 恶意的
  • Violent 暴力的
  • Savage 野蛮的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Vicious" comes from Latin vitiosus, meaning "full of faults or defects," related to vitium, meaning "fault or vice." Over time, it evolved to mean cruel or morally wrong.
vicious 一词源自拉丁语 vitiosus，意思是“充满缺陷的”，与 vitium（缺点，恶习）有关，后来演变为“恶毒的”或“道德败坏的”。
