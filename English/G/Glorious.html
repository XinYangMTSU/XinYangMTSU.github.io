🌟 glorious /ˈɡlɔːriəs/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Glorious describes something that is magnificent, wonderful, or worthy of admiration and praise. It often suggests splendor, beauty, or honor.
glorious 的意思是“辉煌的、壮丽的、值得赞美的”，常用来形容壮观、美丽或带有荣耀感的事物。

Example: They celebrated a glorious victory in the championship.
例句：他们庆祝了在冠军赛中辉煌的胜利。
Example: The sunset over the mountains was absolutely glorious.
例句：山上的日落美得令人赞叹。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Magnificent 壮丽的
  • Splendid 辉煌的
  • Majestic 庄严的
  • Radiant 光辉的
  • Illustrious 杰出的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Glorious" comes from Latin gloriosus, meaning "full of glory" or "famous," from gloria (glory).
glorious 源自拉丁语 gloriosus，意为“充满荣耀的”或“著名的”，来自 gloria（荣耀）。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: gloria / gloriosus – glory, full of glory
拉丁语 gloria / gloriosus：荣耀，光辉
⬇️
Old French: glorieux – glorious, celebrated
古法语 glorieux：光辉的，受赞誉的
⬇️
Modern English: glorious – magnificent, admirable, full of glory
现代英语 glorious：壮丽的，光荣的，值得赞美的
