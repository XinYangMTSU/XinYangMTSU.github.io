🎨 graffiti /ɡrəˈfiːti/

Part of speech: Noun (plural)
词性：名词（复数形式）

📖 Meaning:

Words or drawings scribbled, scratched, or sprayed on walls or other public surfaces, often without permission.
乱写乱画；涂鸦，通常是在公共场所的墙壁或建筑物上未经许可进行的涂写或喷绘。

Example: The walls of the abandoned building were covered in graffiti.
例句：那座废弃建筑的墙上满是涂鸦。
Example: Some people view graffiti as art, while others see it as vandalism.
例句：有些人把涂鸦看作艺术，而另一些人认为那是破坏行为。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Street art 街头艺术
  • Tagging 涂鸦签名
  • Wall painting 墙面绘画
  • Urban art 城市艺术

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Graffiti" comes from the Italian word graffiato, meaning “scratched.” It is the plural of the Italian word graffito, and originally referred to markings found on ancient ruins.
“Graffiti” 来自意大利语 graffiato，意为“划痕、刻痕”。是 graffito 的复数形式，最初指古代遗迹上的刻画。

🧭 Word Evolution

Italian: graffiato – scratched
意大利语 graffiato：刮、刻

Latin: graphium – stylus, to write
拉丁语 graphium：写字的笔

Modern English: graffiti – street art or public markings
现代英语 graffiti：街头涂鸦或公共标记
