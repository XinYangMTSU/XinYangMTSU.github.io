🎞️ genre /ˈʒɑːnrə/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A genre is a category or type of art, music, literature, or film that has a particular style, form, or content.
“genre” 指的是具有特定风格、形式或内容的艺术、音乐、文学或电影的类别。

Example: My favorite movie genre is science fiction.
例句：我最喜欢的电影类型是科幻片。
Example: She reads books from every genre, from romance to mystery.
例句：她各类书都读，从爱情小说到悬疑小说。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Category 类别
  • Type 类型
  • Style 风格
  • Form 形式
  • Classification 分类

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Genre” comes from the French word genre, meaning “kind” or “type,” which in turn comes from Latin genus (meaning “birth, race, kind”).
“genre” 来自法语，意为“种类”，源于拉丁语 genus，表示“出生、种族、类别”。
