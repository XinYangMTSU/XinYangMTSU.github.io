🏞️ Grand Canyon /ɡrænd ˈkænjən/

Part of speech: Proper Noun
词性：专有名词

📖 Meaning:

The Grand Canyon is a massive, steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona, USA. It is known for its immense size and beautiful layered red rock formations.
Grand Canyon（大峡谷）是美国亚利桑那州由科罗拉多河切割而成的巨大峡谷，以其规模宏大和红色岩层的自然美景而著称。

Example: Visiting the Grand Canyon was the highlight of our road trip through the southwest.
例句：参观大峡谷是我们西南自驾游的亮点之一。
Example: The Grand Canyon reveals nearly two billion years of Earth's geological history.
例句：大峡谷展示了地球近二十亿年的地质历史。

🧭 Facts & Geography

  • 📍 Located in Arizona, USA
  • 🌊 Formed by the Colorado River
  • 📏 About 277 miles (446 km) long
  • 📐 Up to 18 miles (29 km) wide and over a mile (1.6 km) deep
  • 🌍 Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • 📍 位于美国亚利桑那州
  • 🌊 由科罗拉多河冲刷形成
  • 📏 长约 446 公里
  • 📐 最宽约 29 公里，最深超 1.6 公里
  • 🌍 被列为联合国教科文组织世界遗产

🌄 Etymology of the Name

📚 The name "Grand Canyon" reflects both the **majestic scale** and **geological depth** of the site. "Canyon" comes from Spanish cañón, meaning "tube" or "gorge."
“Grand Canyon”这个名称体现了其宏伟的规模和深远的地质意义。“Canyon”源自西班牙语“cañón”，意思是“管道”或“峡谷”。
