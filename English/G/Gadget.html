🛠️ gadget /ˈɡædʒɪt/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A gadget is a small device or tool that is clever, often new, and designed to do a specific job.
gadget 指小巧的新奇装置或工具，通常用于执行特定任务。

Example: He loves collecting kitchen gadgets like avocado slicers and garlic presses.
例句：他喜欢收集厨房小工具，比如鳄梨切片器和蒜泥压榨器。
Example: This smart gadget can track your daily steps and heart rate.
例句：这个智能小设备可以记录你的每日步数和心率。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Device 设备
  • Tool 工具
  • Contraption 装置（通常指奇怪或复杂的）
  • Appliance 家用电器
  • Instrument 器具

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "gadget" likely originated in the 19th century, possibly from naval slang for an unnamed mechanical part or tool. Its exact origin is unclear.
gadget 一词可能起源于19世纪，最初是海军俚语，用来指代不知名的机械零件或工具。确切来源尚不明确。
