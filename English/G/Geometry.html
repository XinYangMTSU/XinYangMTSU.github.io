📐 geometry /dʒiˈɑːmətri/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The branch of mathematics that deals with the properties, measurement, and relationships of points, lines, angles, surfaces, and solids.
数学的一个分支，研究点、线、角、面和立体的性质、测量和关系。

Example: She has always enjoyed studying geometry and solving shape-related problems.
例句：她一直喜欢学习几何并解决与形状相关的问题。
Example: Geometry helps architects design buildings with precision.
例句：几何学帮助建筑师精确设计建筑物。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Shape study 形状研究
  • Spatial reasoning 空间推理
  • Mathematical measurement 数学测量

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Geometry" comes from Greek geo meaning "earth" and metron meaning "measure."
geometry 来自希腊语 geo（地球）和 metron（测量）。
