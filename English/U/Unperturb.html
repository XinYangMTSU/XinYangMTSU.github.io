🧘 unperturb /ˌʌn.pəˈtɜːbd/

Part of speech: Adjective (used as past participle)
词性：形容词（过去分词形式）

📖 Meaning:

Unperturbed means not worried, upset, or disturbed by something that might normally cause concern or agitation.
“unperturbed” 意为不受干扰、不烦恼、不担忧，面对可能引起焦虑的事物保持镇定。

Example: She remained unperturbed by the criticism.
例句：她对批评毫不在意，保持冷静。
Example: Despite the chaos, he sat unperturbed in his chair.
例句：尽管现场一片混乱，他仍安坐如常。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Calm 冷静的
  • Composed 沉着的
  • Untroubled 不受困扰的
  • Serene 安详的
  • Undisturbed 未被打扰的

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Unperturbed" is formed from the prefix un- (meaning "not") and perturbed, which comes from Latin perturbare, meaning "to confuse or agitate."
“unperturbed” 由前缀 un-（不）与“perturbed”（烦扰的）组成，后者源自拉丁语 perturbare，意为“使混乱、使烦扰”。
