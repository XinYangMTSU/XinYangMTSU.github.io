👨‍👩‍👧 upbringing /ˈʌpˌbrɪŋɪŋ/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Upbringing refers to the way a child is raised and taught by their parents or caregivers, including values, behaviors, and education.
upbringing 的意思是“一个孩子的成长过程，尤其是父母或看护人对其价值观、行为习惯和教育的影响”。

Example: Her strict upbringing shaped her disciplined personality.
例句：她严格的成长经历塑造了她有纪律的个性。
Example: They gave their children a very liberal upbringing.
例句：他们对孩子的教养非常宽松。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Child-rearing 养育孩子
  • Parenting 育儿
  • Education 教育
  • Training 训练
  • Nurturing 培养

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Upbringing" comes from the verb phrase “bring up,” meaning “to raise a child,” combined with the noun-forming suffix “-ing.”
upbringing 源自动词短语 bring up（养育、抚养）加上名词后缀 -ing。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: bringan – to bring or raise
古英语 bringan：带来、抚养
⬇️
Middle English: upbringen – to raise or nurture
中古英语 upbringen：养育、培养
⬇️
Modern English: upbringing – the process of raising a child
现代英语 upbringing：抚养孩子的过程
