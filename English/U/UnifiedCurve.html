📈 unified curve /ˈjuːnɪfaɪd kɜːrv/

Part of speech: Noun phrase
词性：名词短语

📖 Meaning:

A unified curve refers to a single, smooth line formed by connecting many individual data points. It visually represents a continuous relationship between two variables.
unified curve（统一曲线）是指通过连接多个数据点而形成的一条平滑的曲线，形象地表示两个变量之间的连续关系。

Example: The scattered points from the equation y = x² form a unified curve called a parabola.
例句：来自方程 y = x² 的离散点形成了一条统一的曲线，称为抛物线。
Example: The graph displayed a unified curve that helped students visualize exponential growth.
例句：图表展示了一条统一曲线，帮助学生形象理解指数增长。
