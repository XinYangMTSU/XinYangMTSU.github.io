🛰️ deploy /dɪˈplɔɪ/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To deploy means to move people or equipment into position for action, or to put something into use effectively.
deploy 意为“部署”，指将人员或设备安排到某个位置以投入使用或行动；也可以指有效地使用某种资源。

Example: The troops were deployed to the border region.
例句：部队被部署到了边境地区。
Example: The company plans to deploy new technologies to improve productivity.
例句：公司计划部署新技术以提高生产效率。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Position 安排
  • Station 驻扎
  • Utilize 利用
  • Activate 启动
  • Implement 实施

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Deploy" comes from the French word déployer, meaning "to unfold" or "spread out," originally from Latin displicare — "to scatter" or "unfold."
deploy 来自法语 déployer，意思是“展开”，最初源于拉丁语 displicare，意为“展开”或“分散”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: displicare – to unfold, scatter
拉丁语 displicare：展开，分散
⬇️
Old French: desployer – to unfold
古法语 desployer：展开
⬇️
Modern English: deploy – to position or put into use
现代英语 deploy：部署或投入使用
