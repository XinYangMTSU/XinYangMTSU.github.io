🧠 deliberately /dɪˈlɪb.ər.ət.li/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

Deliberately means doing something consciously and intentionally, rather than by accident.
“deliberately” 意为有意识地、故意地去做某事，而不是偶然或无意中发生的。

Example: She deliberately avoided eye contact.
例句：她故意避开了眼神接触。
Example: The door was left open deliberately to let the cat in.
例句：门是故意开着的，好让猫进来。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Intentionally 有意地
  • On purpose 故意地
  • Knowingly 明知地
  • Willfully 任意地
  • Calculatedly 蓄意地

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Deliberately" comes from the Latin word deliberare, meaning "to consider carefully," which passed into Old French and then English.
“deliberately” 源自拉丁语 deliberare（慎重考虑），通过古法语传入英语。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
