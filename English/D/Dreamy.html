🌙 dreamy /ˈdriːmi/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Dreamy describes something that is magical, gentle, peaceful, or has a romantic or unreal quality, like in a dream.
“dreamy” 描述的是梦幻般的、温柔的、宁静的，或带有浪漫、不真实的特质。

Example: The music had a soft, dreamy quality that made everyone relax.
例句：这段音乐带有柔和梦幻的特质，让人放松下来。
Example: She stared out the window with a dreamy expression on her face.
例句：她面带梦幻的表情望向窗外。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Whimsical 异想天开的
  • Romantic 浪漫的
  • Airy 轻盈的
  • Fantasy-like 幻想般的
  • Peaceful 宁静的

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Dreamy” comes from the word “dream,” which originates from Old English *drēam* meaning “joy” or “music,” later evolving to refer to imagined visions during sleep. “Dreamy” has come to mean “like a dream.”
“dreamy” 源自 “dream”，其古英语 *drēam* 原意为“欢乐”或“音乐”，后来演变为“梦境”。“dreamy” 则指“像梦一样的”。
