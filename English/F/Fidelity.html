🎧 fidelity /fɪˈdɛlɪti/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Fidelity has two main meanings:

  1. Loyalty and faithfulness to a person, cause, or belief.
  2. Accuracy in reproduction—how closely something matches the original (such as sound, image, or translation).
“fidelity” 有两个主要含义：
1. 对某人、事业或信仰的忠诚；
2. 再现的准确性，如音质、图像或翻译的逼真度。

Example: She was admired for her fidelity to her values.
例句：她因忠于自己的价值观而受到尊敬。
Example: This speaker system offers high fidelity audio reproduction.
例句：这款音响系统提供高保真的音频还原。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Loyalty 忠诚
  • Faithfulness 忠实
  • Accuracy 准确性
  • Exactness 精确性
  • Authenticity 真实性

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Fidelity” comes from Latin fidelitas, from fidelis meaning “faithful,” which in turn comes from fides meaning “faith.” It originally referred to loyalty and trustworthiness.
“fidelity” 来自拉丁语 fidelitas，源自 fidelis（忠诚的），再追溯至 fides（信任、信仰）。最初用于表示忠诚与可信赖。
