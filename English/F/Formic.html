🐜 formic /ˈfɔːrmɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Formic relates to or is derived from ants, especially referring to **formic acid**, which is found in ant venom.
formic 意思是“与蚂蚁有关的”或“源自动物蚁类的”，尤其是指蚁酸（formic acid），一种存在于蚂蚁毒液中的物质。

Example: The sting of the ant releases formic acid, causing a burning sensation.
例句：蚂蚁的叮咬会释放蚁酸，造成灼热感。
Example: Scientists studied the formic compounds in ant venom for medicinal purposes.
例句：科学家研究蚂蚁毒液中的 formic 化合物用于医学目的。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Ant-related 与蚂蚁有关的
  • Myrmecological 蚁类学的
  • Venomous 带毒液的（特指昆虫）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Formic" comes from Latin formica, meaning "ant." The chemical compound formic acid was first isolated from ants, hence the name.
formic 来自拉丁语 formica，意思是“蚂蚁”。蚁酸（formic acid）最初就是从蚂蚁体内分离出来的，因此得名。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: formica – ant
拉丁语 formica：蚂蚁
⬇️
Scientific Latin: formica acidum – ant acid (formic acid)
科学拉丁语 formica acidum：蚁酸
⬇️
Modern English: formic – related to ants or ant acid
现代英语 formic：与蚂蚁或蚁酸相关
