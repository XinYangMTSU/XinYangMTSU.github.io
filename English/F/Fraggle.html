🎭 Fraggle /ˈfræɡəl/

Part of speech: Proper Noun
词性：专有名词

📖 Meaning:

A Fraggle is one of the main characters in the children’s television series Fraggle Rock, created by Jim Henson. Fraggles are colorful, curious creatures who live in a magical underground world and explore themes of friendship, cooperation, and diversity.
Fraggle 是由吉姆·汉森创作的儿童电视剧《Fraggle Rock》中的主角之一。这些色彩鲜艳、好奇的生物生活在一个神奇的地下世界，探讨友情、合作和多样性等主题。

Example: Gobo is one of the most adventurous Fraggles in Fraggle Rock.
例句：Gobo 是《Fraggle Rock》中最具冒险精神的 Fraggle 之一。
Example: The Fraggles sang songs to express their emotions and resolve conflicts.
例句：Fraggles 用歌声表达情感并解决冲突。

🎬 Series Background

📺 Fraggle Rock premiered in 1983 as a puppet-based TV show that aimed to entertain and teach important social lessons through musical storytelling and imaginative world-building.
《Fraggle Rock》首播于1983年，是一部以木偶表演为特色的电视剧，通过音乐和奇幻的世界观传达重要的社会教育理念。

🧭 Cultural Impact

Fraggles became symbols of joy, community, and curiosity. The show emphasized harmony between different communities like the Fraggles, Doozers, and Gorgs.
Fraggle 成为了快乐、社群和好奇心的象征。该剧强调 Fraggles、Doozers 和 Gorgs 等不同群体之间的和谐共处。
