🍫 fudgy /ˈfʌdʒi/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Having a dense, soft, and rich texture similar to fudge, often used to describe brownies or chocolate desserts.
像软糖一样浓稠柔软的；（多用来形容巧克力布朗尼或甜点）。

Example: I prefer brownies that are fudgy rather than cakey.
例句：我更喜欢口感浓稠的布朗尼，而不是像蛋糕一样松软的那种。
Example: The center of the cake was deliciously fudgy.
例句：蛋糕的中心部分浓郁柔软，非常美味。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Rich 浓郁的
  • Dense 浓密的；稠密的
  • Chewy 有嚼劲的
  • Moist 湿润的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Fudgy" is derived from the noun “fudge,” a soft, sweet chocolate confection, combined with the suffix “-y,” which turns it into an adjective describing a texture similar to fudge.
“Fudgy” 源自动词 fudge（巧克力软糖），加上形容词后缀 -y，表示“像软糖一样的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

English: fudge – a rich, soft candy made of sugar, butter, and milk
英语 fudge：由糖、奶油和牛奶制成的浓郁软糖

Modern Usage: fudgy – having the rich, soft qualities of fudge
现代用法 fudgy：具有软糖般浓稠质感的
