🎉 festivity /fɛˈstɪv.ə.ti/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A festivity is a celebration or a joyful event, often involving parties, dancing, or other enjoyable activities.
festivity 指的是庆祝活动，常包含派对、跳舞或其他快乐的事情。

Example: The city was full of festivity during the New Year celebrations.
例句：新年庆祝期间，城市洋溢着节日的喜庆。
Example: The wedding was followed by two days of festivity.
例句：婚礼之后有两天的庆祝活动。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Celebration 庆祝
  • Party 聚会
  • Merrymaking 欢乐活动
  • Jubilation 欢腾
  • Revelry 狂欢

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Festivity" comes from Latin festivitas, meaning "feast or festal celebration," from festivus (festive, joyful).
festivity 来自拉丁语 festivitas，意为“宴会或节日庆祝”，源于 festivus（喜庆的）。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: festivitas – celebration, feast
拉丁语 festivitas：庆典，宴会
⬇️
Old French: festiuité – mirth, festival
古法语 festiuité：欢乐，节日
⬇️
English: festivity – joyful celebration
英语 festivity：喜庆活动

📊 Comparison
