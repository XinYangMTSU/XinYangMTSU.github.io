🔥 fervent /ˈfɜːrvənt/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Displaying passionate intensity or enthusiasm; having or showing great warmth of spirit or strong belief.
热情的；充满激情的；强烈的信仰或情感。

Example: She is a fervent supporter of climate action.
例句：她是气候行动的热情支持者。
Example: He made a fervent plea for peace.
例句：他热情地呼吁和平。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Passionate 热情的
  • Zealous 狂热的
  • Ardent 炽热的；热心的
  • Fiery 炽热的；激烈的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Fervent" comes from Latin fervere, meaning "to boil" or "to glow." It originally described physical heat and came to refer to emotional or spiritual intensity.
“Fervent” 源自拉丁语 fervere，意为“沸腾”或“发热”，最初指物理上的热度，后来引申为情感或精神的炽热。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: fervere – to boil or glow
拉丁语 fervere：沸腾、发热

Old French: fervent – passionate
古法语 fervent：热情的

Modern English: fervent – emotionally intense
现代英语 fervent：情感炽热的
