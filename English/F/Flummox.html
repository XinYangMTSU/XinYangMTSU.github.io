😕 flummox /ˈflʌm.əks/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Flummox means to confuse or bewilder someone completely.
“flummox” 的意思是让某人彻底困惑或不知所措。

Example: The complex instructions flummoxed the new employee.
例句：复杂的操作说明让新员工彻底懵了。
Example: I was flummoxed by the unexpected question.
例句：我被那个意外的问题搞得不知所措。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Confuse 使困惑
  • Bewilder 使迷惑
  • Baffle 使困惑
  • Perplex 使费解
  • Stump 难倒

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Flummox" likely originated in British dialect in the 19th century, possibly imitative or expressive in nature, used to convey confusion.
“flummox” 很可能起源于19世纪英国方言，是一个拟声或感情色彩很强的词，表示困惑。
