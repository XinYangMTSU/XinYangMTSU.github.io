🌟 Frisbee /ˈfrɪz.bi/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Meaning:

A Frisbee is a flat, round plastic disc used for throwing and catching in outdoor games and sports. It glides through the air when spun and is often played with at parks, beaches, and fields.

Example: The kids tossed the frisbee back and forth across the sunny park.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Frisbee
  • Flying disc
  • Throwing disc
  • Glider

🖼️ Image of a Frisbee

📸 A group of people playing with a Frisbee in a field.

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The name "Frisbee" originated from the Frisbie Pie Company in the U.S., whose pie tins college students used to toss around for fun.

📝 The plastic disc version was created by Wham-O in the 1950s and marketed as a toy.

💡 Though "Firsbee" is a common misspelling, it refers to the same flying toy that has become a cultural icon of outdoor play.
