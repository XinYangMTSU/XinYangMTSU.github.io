🔗 inextricably /ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbli/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

Inextricably means in a way that is so closely linked or involved that it is impossible to separate.
“inextricably” 意为紧密相连到无法分开的程度。

Example: The history of the two countries is inextricably linked.
例句：这两个国家的历史密不可分。
Example: Science and mathematics are inextricably connected in many fields.
例句：科学与数学在许多领域中紧密相连，无法分割。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Closely 紧密地
  • Indivisibly 不可分割地
  • Firmly 牢固地
  • Tightly 紧紧地
  • Unbreakably 无法分开的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Inextricably” comes from the Latin word inextricabilis, meaning “that cannot be untangled.” It is formed by the prefix in- (not) + extricabilis (able to untangle).
“inextricably” 来自拉丁语 inextricabilis，意为“无法解开的”。由 in-（否定）+ extricabilis（可解开的）构成。
