❓ inquiry /ˈɪn.kwə.ri/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Inquiry is the act of asking questions or seeking information, often in a formal or systematic way.
“inquiry” 指的是提问或系统性地寻求信息的行为，通常用于正式场合。

Example: The school launched an inquiry into the incident.
例句：学校对这一事件展开了调查。
Example: She made an inquiry about the course schedule.
例句：她询问了课程安排。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Investigation 调查
  • Examination 审查
  • Questioning 质询
  • Query 询问
  • Probe 探查

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Inquiry” comes from the Latin *inquirere*, meaning “to seek” or “to ask,” formed from *in-* (into) + *quaerere* (to ask, seek).
“inquiry” 来自拉丁语 *inquirere*，意思是“寻求”或“询问”，由 *in-*（向内）和 *quaerere*（询问）组成。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
Inquiry Act of seeking information or investigation The company opened an internal inquiry. Academic, legal, business
Question A sentence to ask for information He asked a question about the policy. Everyday conversation
Research Systematic study or investigation Her research focused on climate change. Academic, scientific
