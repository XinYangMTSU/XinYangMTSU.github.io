🌟 indispensable /ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Absolutely necessary; essential.
完全必要的；不可或缺的。

Example: Water is indispensable for human survival.
例句：水是人类生存不可或缺的。
Example: Her expertise has made her indispensable to the team.
例句：她的专业知识使她成为团队不可或缺的一员。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Essential 必要的
  • Vital 至关重要的
  • Crucial 关键的
  • Necessary 必需的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Indispensable" comes from Medieval Latin indispensabilis, meaning "that cannot be dispensed with."
indispensable 来自中世纪拉丁语 indispensabilis，意为“不可免除的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Medieval Latin: indispensabilis – cannot be dispensed with
中世纪拉丁语 indispensabilis：不可免除的
⬇️
French: indispensable – essential, necessary
法语 indispensable：必要的
⬇️
Modern English: indispensable – absolutely necessary
现代英语 indispensable：完全必要的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Essential Neutral Exercise is essential for good health. General
Vital Formal Nutrition is vital for children's growth. Serious
Indispensable Formal Technology has become indispensable in education. Authoritative
