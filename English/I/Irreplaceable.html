💎 irreplaceable /ˌɪr.ɪˈpleɪ.sə.bəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Too valuable or special to be replaced if lost or damaged.
过于宝贵或特别，以至于无法替代或替换。

Example: These photos are irreplaceable memories of our childhood.
例句：这些照片是我们童年无法替代的回忆。
Example: She was an irreplaceable member of the team.
例句：她是团队中不可替代的一员。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Unique 独一无二的
  • Invaluable 无价的
  • Unsubstitutable 不可替代的
  • One of a kind 独特的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Irreplaceable" is formed by adding the prefix ir- (not) to replaceable, which comes from Latin replacere, meaning "to put back."
irreplaceable 由前缀 ir-（不）和 replaceable（可替代的）构成，replaceable 来自拉丁语 replacere，意为“放回”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: replacere – to put back
拉丁语 replacere：放回
⬇️
French: remplacer – to substitute
法语 remplacer：替代
⬇️
English: irreplaceable – unable to be replaced
英语 irreplaceable：无法替代的
