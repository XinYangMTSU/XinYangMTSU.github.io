🌀 isometric /ˌaɪ.səˈme.trɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Isometric refers to having equal dimensions or measurements; often used in geometry, graphics, and exercise contexts.
isometric 的意思是“等长的、等尺寸的”，常用于几何图形、绘图视图或静力训练中。

Example: In an isometric drawing, the dimensions are kept to scale without distortion.
例句：在等距绘图中，尺寸按比例缩放且不失真。
Example: Planks are a great example of isometric exercises that strengthen core muscles.
例句：平板支撑是增强核心肌群的很好的静力训练例子。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Equal-length 等长的
  • Symmetrical 对称的
  • Uniform 一致的
  • Proportional 成比例的
  • Geometric 几何的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Isometric" comes from the Greek roots isos meaning "equal" and metron meaning "measure".
isometric 源自希腊语 isos（等的）和 metron（测量），意指“等长的”或“等尺寸的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: isos + metron – equal + measure
希腊语 isos（等的）+ metron（测量）
⬇️
Modern English: isometric – having equal dimensions
现代英语 isometric：等长、等尺寸的
