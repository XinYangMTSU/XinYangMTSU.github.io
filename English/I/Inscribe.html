✍️ inscribe /ɪnˈskraɪb/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Inscribe means to write or carve (words or symbols) on something, often as a permanent record.
“inscribe” 指在某物上写下或刻下文字或符号，通常用于永久性记录。

Example: They inscribed her name on the monument.
例句：他们把她的名字刻在纪念碑上。
Example: The author inscribed a message in the front of the book.
例句：作者在书的首页写下了一段话。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Engrave 雕刻
  • Etch 蚀刻
  • Write 书写
  • Carve 刻写
  • Mark 标记

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Inscribe" comes from Latin inscribere, meaning "to write on," formed from in- (in or on) and scribere (to write).
“inscribe” 源自拉丁语 inscribere，意为“写在……上”，由 in-（在……上）和 scribere（写）构成。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
Inscribe Write or carve on a surface The ring was inscribed with initials. Gifts, monuments, formal records
Engrave Cut or carve deeply They engraved his name on the trophy. Metals, awards
Etch Carve using acid or tools The glass was etched with patterns. Art, design
