🌟 ingenuity /ˌɪndʒəˈnuːəti/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Definition:

Ingenuity is the quality of being clever, original, and inventive — especially in solving problems or finding new ways to do things.

Example: With remarkable ingenuity, the engineers designed a machine that could clean the ocean.

🧩 Synonyms

  • Creativity
  • Inventiveness
  • Cleverness
  • Originality
  • Resourcefulness

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 The word ingenuity comes from the Latin ingenium, meaning "inborn talent, cleverness."

💡 Over time, the word evolved through French as ingéniosité and entered English in the late Middle Ages.
