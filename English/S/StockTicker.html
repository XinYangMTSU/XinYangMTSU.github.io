💹 stock ticker /stɒk ˈtɪkər/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A stock ticker is a display (often digital) that shows real-time updates of stock prices, usually scrolling across a screen.
stock ticker 指股票行情显示器，通常是一个不断滚动的电子屏幕，实时显示股票价格的变动。

Example: The stock ticker showed Apple’s price climbing steadily throughout the morning.
例句：股票行情显示器显示苹果股价整个上午都在稳定上升。
Example: He kept watching the stock ticker on the news channel during lunch.
例句：午餐时他一直在看新闻频道上的股票行情滚动条。

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The term "stock ticker" originally referred to a machine (a ticker tape machine) used in the 19th and early 20th centuries that printed stock prices on a narrow strip of paper (ticker tape).
stock ticker 一词最初指的是 19 至 20 世纪初的报价打字机，它会在细纸带（ticker tape）上打印股票价格。
