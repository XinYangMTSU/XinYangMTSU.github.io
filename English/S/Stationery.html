✉️ stationery /ˈsteɪʃəˌnɛri/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Materials used for writing, such as paper, pens, envelopes, and office supplies.
文具；书写或办公用具，如纸张、钢笔、信封等。

Example: She has a drawer full of beautiful stationery for writing letters.
例句：她有一个抽屉装满了漂亮的信纸用来写信。
Example: The bookstore sells pens, notebooks, and other stationery items.
例句：书店出售钢笔、笔记本及其他文具用品。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Writing supplies 书写用品
  • Office materials 办公用品
  • Paper goods 纸类商品
  • Desk items 办公桌用品

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Stationery" comes from the word "stationer," a term once used for booksellers and those who sold writing materials from fixed stalls (as opposed to traveling vendors).
“Stationery” 来自“stationer”（文具商），这个词原指从固定摊位出售书籍和书写用品的商人。

🧭 Word Evolution

Medieval English: stationer – a person with a fixed shop for selling books
中世纪英语 stationer：在固定地点卖书的人

Modern English: stationery – paper and office supplies
现代英语 stationery：书写及办公用品
