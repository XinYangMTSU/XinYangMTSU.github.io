👑 supremely /suːˈpriːmli/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

To the highest degree; extremely or exceptionally.
极其，非常；最高程度地。

Example: She was supremely confident in her decision.
例句：她对自己的决定极其自信。
Example: The plan was supremely successful.
例句：这个计划非常成功。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Extremely 极其
  • Exceptionally 异常地
  • Highly 非常
  • Overwhelmingly 压倒性地

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Supremely" comes from the adjective "supreme," which originates from Latin supremus, meaning "highest, most elevated."
supremely 来自形容词 supreme，源自拉丁语 supremus，意为“最高的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: supremus – highest
拉丁语 supremus：最高的
⬇️
Middle English: sovereyn – superior
中古英语 sovereyn：优越的
⬇️
Modern English: supreme → supremely – to the highest degree
现代英语 supreme → supremely：最高程度地

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Extremely Neutral He was extremely tired after the trip. General
Exceptionally Formal She performed exceptionally well on the exam. Academic / Professional
Supremely Formal / Emphatic He was supremely confident in his answer. Elevated / Emphatic
