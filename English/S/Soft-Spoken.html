🗣️ soft-spoken /ˌsɔːft ˈspoʊkən/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Speaking in a quiet and gentle voice.
用柔和、安静的声音说话。

Example: He’s a soft-spoken man who rarely raises his voice.
例句：他是一个说话温和的人，很少提高嗓门。
Example: Her soft-spoken demeanor made everyone feel at ease.
例句：她温柔的说话方式让每个人都感到安心。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Gentle 温柔的
  • Quiet 安静的
  • Mild-mannered 温和有礼的
  • Reserved 内向的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Soft-spoken" is a compound adjective formed from "soft" (gentle) and "spoken" (past participle of speak).
soft-spoken 是由 “soft”（柔和）和 “spoken”（speak 的过去分词）组成的复合形容词。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: softe – gentle, agreeable
古英语 softe：温和的
⬇️
Modern English: soft-spoken – speaking gently and quietly
现代英语 soft-spoken：说话温柔安静的
