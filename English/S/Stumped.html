🌟 stumped /stʌmpt/

Part of speech: Adjective (past participle)

📖 Definition:

If you are stumped, you are confused or unable to figure something out.

Example: I was completely stumped by the final puzzle. (= I didn’t know how to solve it.)

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Baffled
  • Puzzled
  • Confused
  • Bewildered
  • Perplexed

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Originally from the noun stump, which refers to the base of a tree left after cutting. The verb form came to mean “to challenge” or “to baffle,” possibly from the idea of being stopped in your tracks like hitting a tree stump.
