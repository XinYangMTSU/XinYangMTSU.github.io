✨ sleeker /ˈsliːkər/

Part of speech: Adjective (comparative form of "sleek")
词性：形容词（sleek 的比较级）

📖 Meaning:

More smooth, shiny, stylish, or elegant in appearance or design.
更加光滑的，更加时尚的，更加优雅的。

Example: The new phone design is sleeker and more compact.
例句：新款手机的设计更加流畅紧凑。
Example: Her hairstyle looked sleeker after using the conditioner.
例句：用了护发素后，她的发型看起来更顺滑了。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Glossier 更有光泽的
  • Smoother 更光滑的
  • More stylish 更时尚的
  • More elegant 更优雅的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Sleeker" is the comparative form of "sleek," which likely comes from Middle English sliken, meaning “to make smooth or glossy.”
sleeker 是 sleek 的比较级，sleek 源自中古英语 sliken，意为“使光滑、有光泽”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Middle English: sliken – to make smooth
中古英语 sliken：使光滑
⬇️
Modern English: sleek – smooth and elegant
现代英语 sleek：光滑而优雅
sleeker – more smooth, stylish, or modern
sleeker：更加光滑、时尚或现代
