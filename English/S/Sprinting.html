🏃‍♂️ sprinting /ˈsprɪntɪŋ/

Part of speech: Verb (present participle)
词性：动词（现在分词）

📖 Meaning:

Running at full speed over a short distance.
以全速短距离奔跑。

Example: He was sprinting to catch the bus.
例句：他在全速奔跑赶公交车。
Example: She kept sprinting until she crossed the finish line.
例句：她一直冲刺到冲过终点线。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Dash 冲刺
  • Race 赛跑
  • Charge 猛冲
  • Run fast 快速奔跑

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Sprint" likely comes from the Old Norse word spretta, meaning “to jump up, burst forth.”
sprint 可能源自古诺斯语 spretta，意为“跳起、突然冲出”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old Norse: spretta – to jump, burst forth
古诺斯语 spretta：跳跃，冲出
⬇️
Middle English: sprinten – to run fast
中古英语 sprinten：快速跑
⬇️
Modern English: sprint – to run at top speed
现代英语 sprint：冲刺、极速奔跑

📊 Comparison

Word Speed Example Use Context
Sprint Very Fast He sprinted the last 100 meters. Short distance / sports
