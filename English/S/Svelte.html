💃 svelte /svɛlt/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Gracefully slender and elegant in appearance or manner.
苗条而优雅的；体态轻盈的。

Example: The actress looked svelte in her designer gown on the red carpet.
例句：那位女演员穿着设计师礼服在红毯上显得苗条优雅。
Example: His svelte figure was the result of years of yoga and mindful eating.
例句：他苗条的身材是多年练瑜伽和注意饮食的结果。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Slender 苗条的
  • Graceful 优雅的
  • Lean 精瘦的
  • Trim 整洁苗条的
  • Willowy 苗条柔美的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Svelte" comes from Italian svelto, meaning “slender, stretched,” the past participle of svellere, meaning “to stretch or pluck out.”
“Svelte” 源自意大利语 svelto，意为“苗条的，拉伸的”，是动词 svellere（拉伸，拔出）的过去分词形式。

🧭 Word Evolution

Italian: svelto – slender, sleek
意大利语 svelto：苗条、灵巧

French (influence): svelte – slender
法语 svelte：纤细的

Modern English: svelte – gracefully slim
现代英语 svelte：苗条优雅的
