🛡️ stout /staʊt/

Part of speech: Adjective / Noun
词性：形容词 / 名词

📖 Meaning:

(adj) Strong, brave, or thick and sturdy in build; (n) A dark, strong type of beer.
（形容词）强壮的，坚定的，厚实的；（名词）黑啤酒的一种，浓烈的啤酒。

Example: He made a stout defense of his beliefs.
例句：他坚定地为自己的信念辩护。
Example: She poured herself a glass of stout to relax.
例句：她倒了一杯黑啤酒来放松一下。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Strong 强壮的
  • Bold 大胆的
  • Robust 健壮的
  • Courageous 有勇气的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Stout" comes from Middle English stout, meaning proud or brave, which originally came from Old French estout and from Germanic roots meaning "proud, bold." Later, it evolved to also mean thick or strong in structure.
“Stout” 来自中古英语 stout，意为“骄傲的”或“勇敢的”，最初源于古法语 estout，最终源自日耳曼语，意为“大胆的”。后来也有“厚实的、结实的”的意思。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old French: estout – bold, proud
古法语 estout：大胆的，骄傲的

Middle English: stout – brave or strong
中古英语 stout：勇敢的，强壮的

Modern English: stout – sturdy or dark beer
现代英语 stout：结实的或黑啤酒
