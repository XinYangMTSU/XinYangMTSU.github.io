🛠️ shovel /ˈʃʌvl/

Part of speech: Noun / Verb
词性：名词 / 动词

📖 Meaning:

As a noun: A tool with a broad flat blade and typically a long handle, used for lifting and moving loose material like soil, snow, or coal.
名词：一种带有宽大扁平刀片和长柄的工具，用于铲土、铲雪或搬运煤炭等松散物体。

As a verb: To lift and move material with a shovel.
动词：用铲子铲起并搬运物体。

Example: He used a shovel to clear snow from the driveway.
例句：他用铲子清理车道上的积雪。
Example: They shoveled dirt into the wheelbarrow.
例句：他们把泥土铲进手推车里。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Spade 铁锹
  • Scoop 铲斗
  • Tool 工具
  • Dig 挖掘（动词）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Shovel" comes from Old English scofl, related to the verb “to shove,” reflecting the pushing motion used when digging or lifting material.
shovel 来自古英语 scofl，与动词 shove（推）相关，反映了使用铲子时的推动动作。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: scofl – a tool for lifting
古英语 scofl：搬运工具
⬇️
Middle English: shovel – tool for digging
中古英语 shovel：用于挖掘的工具
⬇️
Modern English: shovel – to dig or lift with a shovel
现代英语 shovel：铲子，或用铲子铲东西
