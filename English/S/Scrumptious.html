🍰 scrumptious /ˈskrʌmpʃəs/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Extremely tasty or delicious; very appealing to the senses, especially taste.
非常美味的；令人垂涎的。

Example: She baked a scrumptious chocolate cake for the party.
例句：她为派对烤了一块非常美味的巧克力蛋糕。
Example: The food at that restaurant is absolutely scrumptious.
例句：那家餐厅的食物真是太美味了。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Delicious 美味的
  • Tasty 可口的
  • Mouthwatering 令人流口水的
  • Yummy 好吃的（口语）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Scrumptious” is an informal English word, first recorded in the 19th century, possibly an altered form of “sumptuous” (rich and splendid). It reflects delight and sensory pleasure, especially in food.
“scrumptious” 是一个英语口语词，最早记录于19世纪，可能是对“sumptuous”（奢华的）一词的变形，表达对美食的愉悦和享受。

🧭 Word Evolution

Root Idea: Playful and expressive term for delicious food
词根含义：一种描述食物美味的俏皮表达
⬇️
19th Century English: “scrumptious” – tasty and delightful
19世纪英语：“scrumptious” 意为美味可口、令人愉快
⬇️
Modern English: Widely used in casual speech to describe tasty food
现代英语：在日常口语中广泛用于描述好吃的食物
