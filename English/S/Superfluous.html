💬 superfluous /suːˈpɜːrfluəs/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

More than what is needed or necessary; excessive.
多余的；不必要的；过剩的。

Example: Delete any superfluous words from your essay.
例句：请删除作文中所有多余的词语。
Example: The report was filled with superfluous details.
例句：这份报告充满了冗余的细节。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Unnecessary 不必要的
  • Excessive 过多的
  • Redundant 冗余的
  • Extraneous 无关的，多余的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Superfluous" comes from Latin superfluus, meaning "overflowing" or "unnecessary," from super- (over) and fluere (to flow).
superfluous 源自拉丁语 superfluus，意为“多余的、溢出的”，由 super-（超过）和 fluere（流动）组成。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: superfluus – overflowing, unnecessary
拉丁语 superfluus：溢出，不必要的
⬇️
Middle English: superfluous – exceeding what is sufficient
中古英语 superfluous：超出所需的
⬇️
Modern English: superfluous – unnecessary or excessive
现代英语 superfluous：不必要的，过多的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Unnecessary Neutral That explanation is unnecessary. Plain
Redundant Formal This sentence is redundant and should be removed. Critical / Editorial
Superfluous Formal / Elegant The decorations were beautiful but superfluous. Literary / Academic
