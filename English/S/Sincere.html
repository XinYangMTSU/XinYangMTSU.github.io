❤️ sincere /sɪnˈsɪr/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Genuine; expressing true feelings, thoughts, or intentions without pretending.
真诚的；表达真实感情、想法或意图的；不虚伪的。

Example: She gave me a sincere apology for what happened.
例句：她为发生的事情向我真诚地道歉。
Example: I want to express my sincere thanks for your help.
例句：我想表达我对你帮助的真挚感谢。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Genuine 真诚的
  • Honest 诚实的
  • Truthful 真实的
  • Heartfelt 发自内心的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Sincere" comes from Latin sincerus, meaning “clean, pure, sound,” referring to truthfulness or being free from deceit.
sincere 来自拉丁语 sincerus，意为“纯净的、真实的”，引申为不欺骗的真诚之意。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: sincerus – clean, pure, truthful
拉丁语 sincerus：纯净、真实
⬇️
Middle French: sincere – genuine
中古法语 sincere：真诚的
⬇️
Modern English: sincere – honest and heartfelt
现代英语 sincere：诚实且发自内心的

📊 Comparison

Genuine Neutral / Positive Her smile was completely genuine. Warm / Natural
