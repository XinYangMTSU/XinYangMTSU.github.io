🌟 signify /ˈsɪɡnɪˌfaɪ/

Part of speech: Verb

📖 Definition:

To signify means to be a sign of something, to indicate or express a meaning, or to show something clearly.

Example: A red flag on the beach signifies danger in the water.
Example: His silence signified agreement.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Indicate
  • Mean
  • Express
  • Represent
  • Show

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "signify" comes from the Latin significare, which means “to indicate, to point out, to mean.”

✍️ It is formed from signum meaning “sign” and facere meaning “to make.”

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: significare → to indicate, to make a sign
⬇️
Middle English: signifien
⬇️
Modern English: signify
