🧵 spool /spuːl/

Part of speech: Noun / Verb
词性：名词 / 动词

📖 Meaning (as a noun):

A spool is a cylinder on which thread, wire, or film is wound.
spool（名词）是指用于缠绕线、金属丝或胶卷的圆柱形物体。

Example: She found a wooden spool of red thread in her sewing box.
例句：她在针线盒中找到了一卷红线的木线轴。

📖 Meaning (as a verb):

To spool something means to wind it onto or off of a spool.
spool（动词）指的是将某物缠绕或展开在线轴上。

Example: He carefully spooled the tape back into its case.
例句：他小心地将磁带重新卷回盒中。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Reel 卷轴
  • Bobbin 梭芯
  • Roll 卷
  • Coil 线圈
  • Cylinder 圆柱体

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Spool" likely comes from Middle Dutch spoele or Middle Low German spōle, meaning "reel or bobbin."
spool 可能源自中古荷兰语 spoele 或中古低地德语 spōle，意为“卷轴、线轴”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Middle Dutch / Low German: spoele / spōle – reel, bobbin
中古荷兰语 / 低地德语 spoele / spōle：卷轴，线轴
⬇️
Modern English: spool – cylindrical device for winding material
现代英语 spool：用于缠绕材料的圆柱装置
