🌀 solidify /səˈlɪdɪfaɪ/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Solidify means to make or become solid, firm, or definite; to strengthen or reinforce an idea, relationship, or physical substance.
solidify 的意思是“使变固体、使坚固”或“使明确、巩固”。可以用于物理变化，也可以用于抽象概念（如关系、想法的巩固）。

Example: The cooling lava began to solidify into rock.
例句：冷却的熔岩开始凝固成岩石。
Example: Their meeting helped to solidify the partnership between the two companies.
例句：他们的会面有助于巩固双方公司的合作关系。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Harden 变硬
  • Strengthen 加强
  • Cement 巩固
  • Freeze 凝结
  • Stabilize 稳定

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Solidify" comes from Latin solidus meaning “firm” or “solid,” combined with the suffix -ify meaning “to make.” It literally means “to make solid.”
solidify 源自拉丁语 solidus（坚固的）和词缀 -ify（使……），即“使……变得坚固”。
