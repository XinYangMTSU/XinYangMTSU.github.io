🕵️ sleeper agents /ˈsliːpər ˈeɪdʒənts/

Part of speech: Noun (plural)
词性：名词（复数）

📖 Meaning:

Sleeper agents are spies or secret operatives who live in a country pretending to be ordinary citizens, waiting to be activated to carry out a mission.
sleeper agents 指的是潜伏特工，他们伪装成普通人，潜伏在目标国家，等待被激活去执行任务。

Example: The novel featured sleeper agents planted decades earlier by a foreign government.
例句：这本小说描写了几十年前由外国政府安插的潜伏特工。
Example: Like sleeper agents, the students automatically recited the formula when prompted.
例句：就像沉睡特工一样，这些学生一被提问就立刻背出公式。

🧩 Related Terms /rɪˈleɪtɪd/

  • Spy 间谍
  • Operative 特工人员
  • Agent 代理人，特工
  • Undercover 卧底的
  • Espionage 间谍活动

🔍 Origin and Usage /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪn/

📚 The term sleeper agent gained popularity during the Cold War, describing spies who would “sleep” undetected in another country until called into action.
sleeper agent 一词在冷战时期流行开来，描述那些潜伏在他国、直到接到指令才开始行动的间谍。
