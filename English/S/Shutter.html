📸 shutter /ˈʃʌtər/

Part of speech: Noun / Verb
词性：名词 / 动词

📖 Meaning:

Noun: A device that opens and closes to let light into a camera; a cover for a window that blocks light or protects it.
名词：相机的快门；窗户的百叶窗或遮板。

Verb: To close something such as a window or business.
动词：关闭（窗户、商店等）。

Example: The camera shutter clicked as the photo was taken.
例句：拍照时相机的快门咔嗒一声响。
Example: Many shops were shuttered during the storm.
例句：许多商店在暴风雨中关闭了。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Cover 遮盖物
  • Window blind 窗帘
  • Lens gate 镜头挡板
  • Close down (verb) 关闭（动词）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Shutter" comes from the verb "shut," meaning "to close," with the suffix "-er" indicating an object that performs an action. First used in the late 17th century for window coverings, later for cameras.
shutter 来自动词 shut（关闭）和后缀 -er（表示执行动作的物品），最早用于指窗户遮板，后来用于指相机快门。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: scyttan – to shut
古英语 scyttan：关闭
⬇️
Modern English: shutter – window or camera cover
现代英语 shutter：窗户或相机快门
