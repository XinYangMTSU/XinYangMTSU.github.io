💨 streamline /ˈstriːmlaɪn/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To streamline something means to make it more efficient or simpler by removing unnecessary parts or steps.
streamline 的意思是“简化、精简流程”，通过去除不必要的部分使其更高效。

Example: The company streamlined its operations to reduce costs.
例句：公司简化了运营流程以降低成本。
Example: We need to streamline the application process to save time.
例句：我们需要简化申请流程以节省时间。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Simplify 简化
  • Optimize 优化
  • Rationalize 使合理化
  • Trim down 精减
  • Make more efficient 提高效率

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Streamline" originally referred to the smooth shape of objects designed to reduce air or water resistance. It later came to mean making things more efficient in general.
streamline 最初用于描述减少空气或水阻的光滑外形，后来引申为“使流程更高效”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Early 20th Century: Streamline – to give a smooth shape for speed and efficiency
20世纪初：streamline 意为为提高速度和效率而设计的流线形
⬇️
Modern Usage: Streamline – to simplify and make a system more efficient
现代英语：streamline 引申为简化、提高效率

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Simplify Neutral We need to simplify the instructions. Practical
Optimize Technical They optimized the software for faster performance. Technical / professional
Streamline Modern / Business We streamlined our logistics to speed up delivery. Efficient / modern
