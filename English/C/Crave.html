🍫 crave /kreɪv/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To have a strong desire for something, especially something you cannot easily have.
强烈渴望某物，尤其是难以得到的东西。

Example: She craved attention from her parents.
例句：她渴望得到父母的关注。
Example: After a long day, I was craving chocolate.
例句：一天忙下来，我特别想吃巧克力。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Desire 渴望
  • Long for 渴求
  • Yearn 向往
  • Hunger for 渴望，极想

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Crave" comes from Old English crafian, meaning “to ask for” or “demand.”
crave 源自古英语 crafian，意为“请求”或“要求”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: crafian – to demand, ask for
古英语 crafian：要求，请求
⬇️
Middle English: craven – to long for
中古英语 craven：渴望
⬇️
Modern English: crave – to strongly desire
现代英语 crave：强烈渴望

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Desire Neutral He desired peace and quiet. Emotional / Thoughtful
Yearn Formal / Poetic They yearned for their homeland. Romantic / Melancholic
Crave Neutral She craved recognition from her peers. Urgent / Emotional
