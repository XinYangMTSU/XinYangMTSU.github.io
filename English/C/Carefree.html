🌞 carefree /ˈkeə.friː/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Free from worries, responsibilities, or anxiety; feeling or showing a lack of concern.
无忧无虑的；无烦恼的；轻松自在的。

Example: The children laughed and ran through the field in a carefree mood.
例句：孩子们无忧无虑地在田野中奔跑欢笑。
Example: She enjoyed a carefree summer vacation on the beach.
例句：她享受了一个轻松自在的海滩暑假。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Lighthearted 轻松愉快的
  • Untroubled 不受烦扰的
  • Relaxed 放松的
  • Cheerful 快乐的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Carefree" is a compound word formed from “care” (meaning worry or concern) and “free” (meaning without). It first appeared in English in the 16th century to describe someone without burdens or mental stress.
“Carefree” 是由 “care”（烦恼、担忧）和 “free”（无……的）构成的复合词，16 世纪开始用于描述没有烦恼或压力的人。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: caru – care, concern
古英语 caru：关心，忧虑

Middle English: care + fre – free of care
中古英语 care + fre：无忧无虑

Modern English: carefree – without worries or burdens
现代英语 carefree：无忧无虑的
