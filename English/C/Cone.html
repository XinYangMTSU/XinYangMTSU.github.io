🍦 cone /koʊn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A three-dimensional geometric shape with a circular base that tapers smoothly to a single point called the apex or vertex.
圆锥体；一种有圆形底面，并逐渐收拢到顶点的三维几何形状。

Example: An ice cream cone is shaped like a geometric cone.
例句：冰淇淋筒的形状像一个几何圆锥。
Example: Traffic cones are used to direct vehicles away from hazards.
例句：路锥被用来引导车辆避开危险区域。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Cone shape 圆锥形
  • Geometric cone 几何圆锥
  • Tapered solid 锥形体
  • Vertexed shape 有顶点的图形

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Cone" comes from the Latin conus and Greek kōnos, both meaning "pinecone" or "conical shape".
“Cone” 源自拉丁语 conus 和希腊语 kōnos，意思是“松果”或“锥形”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: kōnos – cone, pinecone
希腊语 kōnos：圆锥，松果

Latin: conus – cone
拉丁语 conus：圆锥

Modern English: cone – a shape with a circular base and a pointed top
现代英语 cone：底部圆形、顶部尖的形状
