🧩 composite /ˈkɒmpəzɪt/

Part of speech: Noun / Adjective
词性：名词 / 形容词

📖 Meaning:

Noun: Something made by combining several different parts or materials.
名词：复合物；混合物。

Adjective: Made up of various parts or elements.
形容词：由多种部分组成的；混合的。

Example (noun): The architect created a composite of traditional and modern design.
例句（名词）：建筑师设计了一种传统与现代融合的混合风格。
Example (adjective): A composite material is used in aircraft construction for strength and lightness.
例句（形容词）：飞机建造中使用复合材料以增加强度和减轻重量。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Blend 混合物
  • Mixture 混合体
  • Compound 化合物；复合体
  • Hybrid 混合型；混血的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Composite" comes from the Latin componere, meaning “to put together.” It entered English via French in the 16th century and has been used to describe anything formed by combining multiple components.
“Composite” 来自拉丁语 componere，意为“组合、放在一起”。16 世纪通过法语进入英语，用来描述由多个部分组成的事物。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: componere – to put together
拉丁语 componere：组合，安排

French: composite – made of different parts
法语 composite：由不同部分组成的

Modern English: composite – made of various elements
现代英语 composite：复合的，混合的
