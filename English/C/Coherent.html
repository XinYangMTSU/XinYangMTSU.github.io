🧩 coherent /kəʊˈhɪərənt/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Coherent means clear, logical, and consistent — when all parts of something fit together well and make sense.
coherent 意为“连贯的，一致的，条理清晰的”，表示各部分协调一致，逻辑清楚。

Example: Her essay was well organized and coherent.
例句：她的文章结构良好，条理清晰。
Example: He failed to present a coherent argument during the debate.
例句：在辩论中，他未能提出一个有逻辑的论点。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Logical 逻辑的
  • Consistent 一致的
  • Clear 清晰的
  • Rational 合理的
  • Ordered 有序的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Coherent" comes from Latin cohaerere, meaning "to stick together," from co- (together) + haerere (to stick).
coherent 一词来自拉丁语 cohaerere，意为“粘在一起”，由 co-（一起）和 haerere（粘附）组成。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: cohaerere – to stick together
拉丁语 cohaerere：粘在一起
⬇️
Old French: coherent – consistent, logical
古法语 coherent：一致的，有逻辑的
⬇️
Modern English: coherent – clear and logical
现代英语 coherent：清晰连贯的
