🎎 custom /ˈkʌstəm/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Custom refers to a traditional practice or usual way of behaving in a society, community, or group. It can also refer to something made specifically for an individual.
custom 的意思是“风俗、习惯”，指某社会或群体中传统或普遍的行为方式；也可指“定制的东西”。

Example: It’s their custom to greet guests with tea.
例句：他们有用茶款待客人的习俗。
Example: He ordered a custom suit for the wedding.
例句：他为婚礼定做了一套西装。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Tradition 传统
  • Practice 习俗
  • Habit 习惯
  • Convention 惯例
  • Made-to-order 定制的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Custom" comes from Latin consuetudo meaning "habit or usage," via Old French custume and Middle English custome.
custom 源自拉丁语 consuetudo，意为“习惯、惯例”，经由古法语 custume 和中古英语 custome 演变而来。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: consuetudo – habit or usage
拉丁语 consuetudo：习惯，惯例
⬇️
Old French: custume – custom
古法语 custume：风俗
⬇️
Middle English: custome – a traditional practice
中古英语 custome：传统习俗

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Tradition Formal This festival is rooted in tradition. Cultural / respectful
Habit Neutral Brushing teeth before bed is a healthy habit. Routine
Custom Neutral / Cultural It's a local custom to remove shoes indoors. Descriptive
