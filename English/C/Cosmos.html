🌌 cosmos /ˈkɑːzməs/ or /ˈkɒzmɒs/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Cosmos refers to the universe as an ordered and harmonious system. It suggests not just outer space, but the idea that everything in existence follows an underlying structure or order.
“cosmos” 指宇宙，强调它是一个有秩序、和谐的整体，而不仅仅是星空或太空。

Example: Ancient philosophers believed the cosmos was governed by divine order.
例句：古代哲学家认为宇宙受神圣秩序支配。
Example: The documentary explores the wonders of the cosmos.
例句：那部纪录片探索了宇宙的奇迹。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Universe 宇宙
  • Galaxy 星系
  • Heavens 苍穹；天空
  • Macrocosm 大宇宙
  • Outer space 外太空

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Cosmos” comes from the Greek word kosmos, meaning “order” or “world.” The term was later adopted in Latin and modern English to describe the universe as an ordered whole.
“cosmos” 源自希腊语 “kosmos”，意为“秩序”或“世界”，后来在拉丁语和现代英语中用于描述有序的宇宙整体。
