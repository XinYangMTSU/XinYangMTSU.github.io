🚿 cleanse /klɛnz/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To cleanse means to clean or purify thoroughly, often used figuratively or literally.
cleanse 意为彻底清洁或净化，可以是比喻意义或实际意义。

Example: She cleanses her skin carefully every night.
例句：她每晚都仔细清洁她的皮肤。
Example: People believe certain diets cleanse the body of toxins.
例句：人们相信某些饮食能清除体内的毒素。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Purify 净化
  • Clean 清洁
  • Wash 洗涤
  • Detoxify 排毒

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Cleanse" originates from Old English clænsian, meaning "to purify or make clean."
cleanse 源自古英语 clænsian，意为“净化或使清洁”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: clænsian – to purify, make clean
古英语 clænsian：净化，使清洁
⬇️
Middle English: clensen – to cleanse
中古英语 clensen：清洁
⬇️
Modern English: cleanse – to thoroughly clean or purify
现代英语 cleanse：彻底清洁或净化
