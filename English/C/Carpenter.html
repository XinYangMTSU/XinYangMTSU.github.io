🔨 carpenter /ˈkɑːrpəntər/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A carpenter is a person whose job is to make or repair wooden objects and structures.
carpenter 指的是木匠，即以制作或修理木制物品或结构为职业的人。

Example: The carpenter built a custom bookshelf for the library.
例句：木匠为图书馆打造了一个定制的书架。
Example: He works as a carpenter on construction sites.
例句：他在建筑工地做木工。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Woodworker 木工
  • Craftsman 工匠
  • Joiner 细木工
  • Cabinetmaker 橱柜制作师
  • Builder 建造者

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Carpenter" comes from the Latin word carpentarius, meaning "wagon maker," derived from carpentum, a type of two-wheeled carriage.
carpenter 一词来自拉丁语 carpentarius，原意为“车匠”，源自 carpentum，一种双轮马车。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: carpentarius – carriage maker
拉丁语 carpentarius：马车制造者
⬇️
Old French: carpentier
古法语 carpentier
⬇️
Middle English: carpenter – woodworker
中古英语 carpenter：木工
