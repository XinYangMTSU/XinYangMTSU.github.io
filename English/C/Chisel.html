🪓 chisel /ˈtʃɪzl/

Part of speech: Noun / Verb
词性：名词 / 动词

📖 Meaning:

As a noun: A tool with a sharp edge used to cut or shape wood, stone, or metal.
名词：凿子；一种用于切削或雕刻木头、石头或金属的工具。

As a verb: To cut or shape something using a chisel.
动词：用凿子雕刻或切割。

Example: The sculptor used a chisel to carve the statue’s details.
例句：雕刻家用凿子雕刻雕像的细节。
Example: He carefully chiseled the design into the wooden panel.
例句：他小心地把图案雕刻在木板上。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Carving tool 雕刻工具
  • Sculpting tool 雕塑工具
  • Gouge 凿子（有凹口）
  • Engrave (verb) 雕刻（动词）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Chisel" comes from Old French cisel, from Latin cisellum, meaning "cutting tool", from caedere — "to cut".
“chisel” 来自古法语 cisel，源于拉丁语 cisellum，意为“切割工具”，其动词词根 caedere 表示“切割”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: caedere – to cut
拉丁语 caedere：切割
⬇️
Old French: cisel – carving tool
古法语 cisel：雕刻工具
⬇️
Modern English: chisel – tool or action for cutting/carving
现代英语 chisel：用于切削/雕刻的工具或动作
