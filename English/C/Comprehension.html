🧠 comprehension /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnʃən/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Meaning:

Comprehension means the ability to understand something. It often refers to how well someone grasps meaning from what they read, hear, or experience.

Example: Reading comprehension is a key skill in every subject, not just English.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Understanding
  • Grasp
  • Awareness
  • Interpretation
  • Insight
  • Perception

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Let’s look at the origin (etymology) of the word "comprehension".

📝It comes from the Latin verb comprehendere, meaning "to grasp, seize, or take together."

  • com- = together
  • prehendere = to grasp or seize

💡 So “comprehension” literally means “grasping together” — mentally holding the ideas in your mind to make sense of them.
