📐 curvature /ˈkɜː.və.tʃər/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Curvature refers to the amount or degree to which something is curved or bent, especially in geometry or space.
“curvature” 指的是某物弯曲或曲率的程度，常用于几何或空间中。

Example: The curvature of the Earth becomes visible at high altitudes.
例句：在高空中可以看到地球的弯曲程度。
Example: Einstein’s theory describes how mass causes curvature in space-time.
例句：爱因斯坦的理论描述了质量如何引起时空的弯曲。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Bend 弯曲
  • Arc 弧形
  • Curve 曲线
  • Bow 弓形
  • Flexure 屈曲

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Curvature" comes from the Latin root curvatura, meaning "a bending," from curvus (curved).
“curvature” 源自拉丁语 curvatura，意为“弯曲”，词根是 curvus，意思是“弯的”。
