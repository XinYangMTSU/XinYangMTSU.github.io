🚪 cupboard /ˈkʌbərd/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A piece of furniture or built-in space with doors and shelves used for storing dishes, food, or other household items.
橱柜；用于存放碗碟、食物或其他家庭物品的家具或内置储物空间。

Example: She opened the kitchen cupboard to get a plate.
例句：她打开厨房的橱柜拿了一个盘子。
Example: The cleaning supplies are stored in the bathroom cupboard.
例句：清洁用品放在浴室的橱柜里。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Cabinet 橱柜
  • Closet 衣橱（用于放衣物）
  • Pantry 食品储藏室
  • Shelf 架子

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Cupboard" originated from the words cup + board in Middle English, referring to a board or shelf to hold cups. Over time, it evolved to mean an enclosed storage cabinet.
“Cupboard” 源自中古英语中 “cup”（杯子）和 “board”（板子）的组合，最初指放置杯子的架子，后来演变为带门的储物橱柜。

🧭 Word Evolution

Middle English: cup + board – a board to hold cups
中古英语：cup + board（放杯子的架子）

Modern English: cupboard – enclosed furniture with shelves
现代英语 cupboard：有门的储物橱柜
