☁️ cumulus /ˈkjuːmjələs/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A cumulus cloud is a large, white, fluffy cloud with a flat base, often seen on sunny days.
“cumulus” 指的是一种大而白、形状蓬松、底部平坦的云，常见于晴天。

Example: Cumulus clouds floated across the blue summer sky.
例句：积云在夏日的蓝天中飘荡。
Example: The painting showed a mountain range beneath cumulus clouds.
例句：画中展现了群山之上漂浮着的积云。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Cloud 云
  • Fluffy cloud 蓬松的云
  • Fair-weather cloud 晴天云
  • White puff 白色团团的云
  • Billow 翻滚的云团

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Cumulus” comes from Latin cumulus, meaning “heap” or “pile.” The word reflects the cloud’s shape, as it looks like a **pile of cotton or whipped cream**.
“cumulus” 来自拉丁语 cumulus，意为“堆、团”，形容这种云就像一团堆积的棉花或奶油。

📊 Comparison with Other Cloud Types

Cloud Type Appearance Weather Altitude
Cumulus Fluffy and white with a flat base Fair weather Low
Stratus Gray and flat, covering sky like a blanket Drizzle or overcast Low
Cirrus Thin and wispy Fair but can signal change High
