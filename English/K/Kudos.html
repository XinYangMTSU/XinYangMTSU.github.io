🏆 kudos /ˈkuːdɑːs/ or /ˈkuːdoʊz/

Part of speech: Noun (uncountable)
词性：名词（不可数）

📖 Meaning:

Praise and honor for an achievement.
因成就而获得的赞扬与荣誉。

Example: She received kudos for her outstanding presentation.
例句：她因出色的演讲而受到赞扬。
Example: All the kudos goes to the volunteers who made this event possible.
例句：所有的赞誉都应归功于促成这次活动的志愿者们。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Honor 荣誉
  • Praise 称赞
  • Credit 功劳
  • Acclaim 喝彩

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Kudos" comes from Greek kydos, meaning “glory” or “renown.” It entered English in the 19th century as a singular noun, but is often mistakenly treated as plural in modern usage.
kudos 源自希腊语 kydos，意为“荣耀”或“名声”。19世纪进入英语时是单数名词，但现代英语中常被误认为是复数。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: kydos – glory
希腊语 kydos：荣耀
⬇️
English (19th c.): kudos – praise and honor
英语（19世纪）：kudos：赞扬与荣誉
⬇️
Modern Use: Often mistaken as plural
现代用法：常被误认为是复数形式
