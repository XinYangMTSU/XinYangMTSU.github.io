🌱 nourish /ˈnɜː.rɪʃ/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Nourish means to provide food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition; it can also mean to support or strengthen a feeling, idea, or belief.
“nourish” 指的是提供食物或其他有助于成长、健康和良好状态的物质；也可指滋养、支持某种情感、思想或信念。

Example: This soup will nourish your body during recovery.
例句：这碗汤会在你康复期间滋养你的身体。
Example: She nourished hopes of becoming a great writer.
例句：她怀有成为一名伟大作家的希望。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Feed 喂养
  • Sustain 维持
  • Support 支持
  • Encourage 鼓励
  • Cultivate 培养

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Nourish" comes from Old French nouriss-, from Latin nutrire, meaning "to feed, foster, or support".
“nourish” 源自古法语 nouriss-，其根源是拉丁语 nutrire，意为“喂养、养育、支持”。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
