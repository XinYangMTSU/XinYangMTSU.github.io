🌟 nifty /ˈnɪfti/

Part of speech: Adjective

📖 Meaning:

“Nifty” means something that is particularly good, clever, or stylish—often small and useful in a smart way.

Example: She showed me a nifty little app that organizes your tasks with voice commands.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Cool
  • Neat
  • Handy
  • Stylish
  • Smart
  • Ingenious

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Let’s look at the origin (etymology) of the word "nifty".

📝The exact origin is uncertain, but "nifty" likely emerged in American English in the 19th century as slang for something attractive or clever.

💡 Possibly related to "neat" or "thrifty," it was first documented in the 1860s and grew popular in the 20th century in both American and British informal speech.
