🕰️ nostalgic /nəˈstældʒɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Feeling happy and slightly sad when thinking about the past, especially about a time or place that was happy.
当回忆过去（尤指愉快的时光）时感到快乐又有些忧伤。

Example: Seeing my old school made me feel nostalgic.
例句：看到我以前的学校让我感到怀旧。
Example: He grew nostalgic for his childhood summers by the sea.
例句：他对童年在海边度过的夏天充满怀旧之情。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Sentimental 多愁善感的
  • Wistful 惆怅的
  • Melancholy 忧郁的
  • Yearning 渴望的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Nostalgic" comes from "nostalgia," which was formed from Greek nostos (return home) + algos (pain).
nostalgic 来自单词 nostalgia，该词源自希腊语 nostos（回家）+ algos（痛苦）。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: nostos + algos – homecoming + pain
希腊语 nostos + algos：回家 + 痛苦
⬇️
Modern English: nostalgic – longing for the past
现代英语 nostalgic：对过去的怀念或向往

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone

Wistful Literary He had a wistful smile remembering the past. Longing / Gentle
