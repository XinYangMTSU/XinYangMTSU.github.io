🌐 holistic /hoʊˈlɪstɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Considering something as a whole, rather than just a collection of parts; focusing on the complete system or person, especially in health, education, or analysis.
整体的；全面的；强调整体而非部分的。

Example: A holistic approach to education considers emotional, social, and academic development.
例句：教育的整体方法会考虑情感、社交和学术的发展。
Example: Holistic medicine treats the body, mind, and spirit together.
例句：整体医学将身体、心理和精神作为一个整体来治疗。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Comprehensive 全面的
  • Integrated 整合的
  • Whole-person 以人为整体的
  • All-encompassing 包罗万象的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Holistic" comes from the Greek word holos, meaning “whole.” It was first used in the early 20th century in English, especially in philosophical and medical contexts.
“Holistic” 源自希腊语 holos，意为“整体”。20 世纪初首次进入英语，常用于哲学和医学领域。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: holos – whole, entire
希腊语 holos：整体，全部

Modern English: holistic – relating to the whole rather than parts
现代英语 holistic：着眼于整体的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
