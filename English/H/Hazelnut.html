🌰 hazelnut /ˈheɪzəlˌnʌt/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A round brown nut with a hard shell, often used in chocolate, spreads, or desserts; the nut of the hazel tree.
榛子；榛树的坚果，常用于巧克力、甜点或涂抹酱中。

Example: I love the flavor of hazelnut in my coffee.
例句：我喜欢榛子味的咖啡。
Example: The cake was topped with crushed hazelnuts.
例句：蛋糕上撒了碎榛子。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Nut 坚果
  • Filbert (another name for hazelnut) 榛子（另一种说法）
  • Seed (in botanical terms) 种子（植物学用语）
  • Hazel fruit 榛果

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Hazelnut" comes from Old English hæselhnutu, a compound of "hazel" (the tree) and "nut". It has been used for centuries in European cuisine.
“hazelnut” 源自古英语 hæselhnutu，是 hazel（榛树）和 nut（坚果）的复合词，几个世纪以来在欧洲饮食中广泛使用。
