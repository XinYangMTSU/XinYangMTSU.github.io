🐴 harness /ˈhɑːrnəs/

Part of speech: Verb / Noun
词性：动词 / 名词

📖 Meaning:

Verb: To control and make use of a natural resource or power.
动词：控制并利用（自然资源或能量）。

Noun: A set of straps used to attach or control an animal or secure a person.
名词：用于控制动物或固定人的一组带子；安全带。

Example: Engineers are working to harness solar energy more efficiently.
例句：工程师正在努力更高效地利用太阳能。
Example: The climber adjusted his harness before starting the ascent.
例句：登山者在开始攀登前调整了他的安全带。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Utilize 利用
  • Control 控制
  • Exploit 开发；利用
  • Apply 应用

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Harness" comes from Old French harneis, meaning “equipment or gear,” especially for a horse, and is of Germanic origin.
harness 来自古法语 harneis，意为“装备”或“马具”，源自日耳曼语。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old French: harneis – equipment, armor
古法语 harneis：装备，盔甲
⬇️
Modern English: harness – strap device or to utilize
现代英语 harness：安全带装置；利用资源

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Utilize Formal We must utilize available data for better insights. Technical
Exploit Neutral / Negative The company exploits renewable energy sources. Neutral / Critical
Harness Neutral We need to harness wind power for sustainability. Scientific / Practical
