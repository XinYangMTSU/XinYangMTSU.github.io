⬡ hexagon /ˈhɛksəˌɡɒn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A flat, two-dimensional shape with six straight sides and six angles.
六边形；一个有六条直边和六个角的平面图形。

Example: A beehive is made up of perfectly arranged hexagons.
例句：蜂巢是由排列整齐的六边形构成的。
Example: A regular hexagon has all sides and angles equal.
例句：一个正六边形的所有边和角都相等。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Six-sided shape 六边形
  • Hexagram (if star-shaped) 六角星（如果是星形）
  • Geometric figure 几何图形

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Hexagon" comes from Greek hex meaning "six" and -gon meaning "angle" or "corner".
“Hexagon” 来自希腊语：前缀 hex 表示“六”，后缀 -gon 表示“角”或“边”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: hexagōnos – six-angled
希腊语 hexagōnos：六个角的

Late Latin: hexagonum – six-sided figure
晚期拉丁语 hexagonum：六边形

Modern English: hexagon – a 2D shape with six sides
现代英语 hexagon：六边形
