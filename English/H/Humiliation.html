🌟 humiliation /hjuːˌmɪliˈeɪʃn/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Meaning:

Humiliation means the feeling of being ashamed or losing respect, usually in a public or embarrassing way.

Example: He felt deep humiliation after forgetting his lines in front of the whole class.

🧹 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Embarrassment
  • Shame
  • Disgrace
  • Mortification
  • Degradation
  • Loss of face

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "humiliation" comes from the Latin verb humiliare, which means "to humble" or "to bring low".

  • humilis = low, humble
  • -tion = noun suffix meaning the act or process of

🖍️ "Humiliation" literally means the act of making someone feel low or bringing down their dignity.
