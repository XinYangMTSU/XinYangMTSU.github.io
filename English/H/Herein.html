📍 herein /ˌhɪərˈɪn/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

Herein means “in this document, statement, or situation.” It’s often used in formal or legal writing.
“herein” 的意思是“在此（文件、陈述或情境中）”，常用于正式或法律文体中。

Example: The terms outlined herein are binding upon all parties.
例句：此处列明的条款对所有当事方具有约束力。
Example: Herein lies the problem with their argument.
例句：问题就在于他们的论点中。

🧩 Synonyms

  • In this 在此
  • Within 之内
  • Inside 内部
  • Contained 所包含的
  • Included 包括在内的

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Herein” is formed from “here” + “in,” and dates back to Middle English, used especially in formal, legal, or academic contexts.
“herein” 源自 “here” 与 “in” 的组合，最早见于中古英语，常用于正式、法律或学术语境中。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
