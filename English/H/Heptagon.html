🟦 heptagon /ˈhɛptəˌɡɒn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A flat, two-dimensional shape with seven straight sides and seven angles.
七边形；一个有七条边和七个角的平面图形。

Example: A regular heptagon has seven equal sides and seven equal angles.
例句：正七边形的七条边和七个角都相等。
Example: It's rare to find heptagons used in everyday architecture.
例句：在日常建筑中很少使用七边形。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Seven-sided shape 七边形
  • Polygon 多边形
  • Heptagonal figure 七边图形

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Heptagon" comes from Greek hepta meaning "seven" and -gon meaning "angle" or "corner".
“Heptagon” 来自希腊语：hepta 表示“七”，-gon 表示“角”或“边”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: heptagōnos – seven-angled
希腊语 heptagōnos：七个角的

Late Latin: heptagonum – seven-sided figure
晚期拉丁语 heptagonum：七边形

Modern English: heptagon – a polygon with seven sides
现代英语 heptagon：七边形
