🌍 humanity /hjuːˈmænəti/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

1. All human beings collectively; the human race.
2. Kindness, compassion, and benevolence toward others.
1. 人类；全体人类。
2. 人性；仁慈；善良。

Example: Climate change is a serious challenge facing all of humanity.
例句：气候变化是全人类面临的一个严峻挑战。
Example: Her act of kindness restored his faith in humanity.
例句：她的善举让他重新相信人性。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Mankind 人类
  • Compassion 同情；怜悯
  • Kindness 善良；仁慈
  • Benevolence 仁爱；慈善

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Humanity" comes from Latin humanitas, meaning "human nature, kindness," from humanus meaning "human."
“Humanity” 源自拉丁语 humanitas，意为“人性、仁慈”，来自 humanus，意为“人的、人类的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: humanus → humanitas – human nature, kindness
拉丁语 humanus → humanitas：人性，仁爱

Middle English: humanite – human character or kindness
中古英语 humanite：人的特性或仁慈

Modern English: humanity – human beings or human compassion
现代英语 humanity：人类；人性；仁爱

📊 Comparison
