🔍 minutiae /maɪˈnuːʃiˌiː/

Part of speech: Noun (plural)
词性：名词（复数）

📖 Meaning:

Minutiae refers to the small, precise, or trivial details of something.
minutiae 意为“细节、小而琐碎的事项”，指的是事物中非常微小、具体的部分。

Example: He was overwhelmed by the minutiae of everyday life.
例句：他被日常生活的琐碎细节压得喘不过气。
Example: The lawyer examined all the contract's minutiae before signing.
例句：律师在签字前仔细检查了合同中的每一个细节。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Details 细节
  • Trivialities 琐事
  • Nuances 细微差别
  • Particulars 具体事项
  • Fine points 细节要点

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Minutiae" is the plural of the Latin word minutia, meaning "smallness" or "trifling detail", from minutus meaning "small".
minutiae 是拉丁语 minutia 的复数形式，意为“微小或琐碎的细节”，源自动词 minutus，意为“小的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: minutus – small
拉丁语 minutus：小的
⬇️
Late Latin: minutia – a small detail
拉丁语 minutia：细小的事物
⬇️
Modern English: minutiae – small, precise details
现代英语 minutiae：细节，琐事

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Details Neutral She explained all the details of the plan. Clear / explanatory
Trivialities Formal He ignored the trivialities and focused on the main task. Slightly dismissive
Minutiae Formal / Academic The historian studied the minutiae of ancient documents. Precise / intellectual
