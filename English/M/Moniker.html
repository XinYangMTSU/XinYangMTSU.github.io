🏷️ moniker /ˈmɑː.nɪ.kɚ/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A moniker is a name or nickname that someone or something is known by.
moniker 指的是某人或某物的名字或绰号。

Example: The singer performs under the moniker "Starlight".
例句：这位歌手以“Starlight”的名字演出。
Example: His real name is Jonathan, but he goes by the moniker "J-Dog".
例句：他本名是乔纳森，但人们叫他“J-Dog”。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Nickname 昵称
  • Alias 别名
  • Label 标签
  • Name 名字
  • Tag 称号

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word moniker is believed to have originated in American slang in the 19th century, possibly from Irish or Scottish roots meaning "name."
moniker 一词可能起源于19世纪的美式俚语，可能与爱尔兰或苏格兰表示“名字”的词有关。

🧭 Word Evolution

Early usage: moniker – informal name or alias
早期用法：moniker 表示非正式的名字或别称
