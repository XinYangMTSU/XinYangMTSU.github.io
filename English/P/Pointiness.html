🔺 pointiness /ˈpɔɪnti.nəs/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The quality of having a sharp or well-defined point or points. It often describes the shape or design of an object that appears angular or spiky.
尖锐；尖角特性；具有明显尖端的状态。

Example: The pointiness of the star made it stand out in the design.
例句：星形的尖角让它在设计中脱颖而出。
Example: The cactus’s pointiness warns animals to keep their distance.
例句：仙人掌的尖刺警示动物不要靠近。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Sharpness 尖锐
  • Angularity 角度感
  • Edginess 棱角感
  • Spikiness 刺状；尖刺性

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Pointiness" is a noun formed by adding the suffix -ness to the adjective pointy, which derives from the noun point (meaning a sharp tip or end). It is a relatively modern, informal term used to describe visual or physical sharpness.
“Pointiness” 是在形容词 pointy（尖的）后加上名词后缀 -ness 构成的，来源于名词 point（尖端）。这是一个比较现代且非正式的词，用于描述外观或触感上的尖锐性。

🧭 Word Formation

Root: point – a sharp end
词根：point（尖端）

Adjective: pointy – having a sharp point
形容词：pointy（尖的）

Noun: pointiness – the state of being pointy
名词：pointiness（尖锐性）
