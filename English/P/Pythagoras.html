📐 Pythagoras /pɪˈθæɡərəs/

Part of speech: Proper Noun (Person)
词性：专有名词（人物）

📖 Meaning:

Pythagoras was an ancient Greek philosopher and mathematician best known for the **Pythagorean Theorem**, which states that in a right-angled triangle, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of the squares of the other two sides.
毕达哥拉斯是古希腊哲学家和数学家，以“毕达哥拉斯定理”著称：直角三角形中，斜边的平方等于另外两边平方之和。

Example: Pythagoras laid the foundation for much of modern geometry.
例句：毕达哥拉斯为现代几何学奠定了基础。
Example: The Pythagorean Theorem is named after Pythagoras.
例句：毕达哥拉斯定理以毕达哥拉斯的名字命名。

🧩 Related Concepts /ˈkɒnsɛpts/

  • Pythagorean Theorem 毕达哥拉斯定理
  • Triangle 三角形
  • Right Angle 直角
  • Mathematics and Harmony 数学与和谐理念

🔍 Etymology and Background /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Pythagoras" comes from the Greek name Πυθαγόρας. He lived around 570–495 BCE and founded a philosophical school that believed numbers and mathematical relationships were fundamental to understanding the universe.
“Pythagoras” 来自希腊语 Πυθαγόρας。他生活在公元前 570 到 495 年左右，创立了一个哲学学派，认为数字与数学关系是理解宇宙的基础。

🧭 Historical Significance

Birthplace: Samos, Greece
出生地：希腊萨摩斯岛

Legacy: Pythagorean Theorem, influence on geometry, philosophy, and musical theory
遗产：毕达哥拉斯定理，对几何学、哲学和音乐理论的影响深远
