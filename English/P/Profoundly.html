🌌 profoundly /prəˈfaʊnd.li/

Part of speech: Adverb
词性：副词

📖 Meaning:

Profoundly means to a very great or intense degree; deeply and meaningfully.
“profoundly” 意为“极大程度地；深刻地；意义重大地”。

Example: The discovery profoundly changed our understanding of the universe.
例句：这一发现极大地改变了我们对宇宙的认识。
Example: She was profoundly moved by the speech.
例句：她被这场演讲深深打动。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Deeply 深深地
  • Greatly 极大地
  • Seriously 严重地；严肃地
  • Meaningfully 有意义地
  • Extremely 非常

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Profoundly" comes from the adjective "profound," which is derived from Latin profundus, meaning “deep.”
“profoundly” 源自动词“profound”（深刻的），其词根是拉丁语 profundus，意思是“深的”。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
Profoundly To a deep or intense degree The news profoundly affected her. Emotional, intellectual
Deeply Far below the surface; strongly felt He was deeply grateful. Emotional expression
