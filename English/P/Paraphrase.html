🔁 paraphrase /ˈpærəˌfreɪz/

Part of speech: Verb or Noun
词性：动词或名词

📖 As a verb (action):

To paraphrase something means to restate it in your own words—usually to make it simpler or clearer while keeping the original meaning.
paraphrase（动词）意思是用你自己的话重新表达原意，通常是为了让它更清楚或更简单。

Example: Let me paraphrase what she said: "We need to try harder."
例句：让我用自己的话转述她的话：“我们需要更努力。”

📖 As a noun (a thing):

A paraphrase is the version you created—your restated sentence or explanation.
paraphrase（名词）指的是你所写的那句重新表述的句子或解释。

Example: His paraphrase of the poem helped younger students understand its meaning.
例句：他对这首诗的转述帮助了年幼的学生理解它的含义。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Restate 重述
  • Reword 改写
  • Summarize 总结
  • Interpret 解释
  • Explain in other words 换句话说

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "paraphrase" comes from the Latin and Greek word paraphrasis, meaning "a rewording."
paraphrase 一词来自拉丁语和希腊语 paraphrasis，意思是“重新表达”。

  • para- = beside, alongside 在旁边，靠近
  • phrasis = speech or expression 表达，说话

💡 So to paraphrase literally means to "say something beside"—in other words, to say the same thing in different words.
所以 paraphrase 的字面意思是“在旁边说话”，引申为“换一种说法表达同样的意思”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: paraphrasis – rewording, explanation /paˈra.fra.sis/
希腊语 paraphrasis：重新表达，解释
⬇️
Latin: paraphrasis – explanation in other words
拉丁语 paraphrasis：用其他词语解释
⬇️
English: paraphrase /ˈpærəˌfreɪz/
英语：paraphrase，用于表达转述、改写
