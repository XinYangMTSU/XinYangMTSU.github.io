🌟 pedagogy /ˈpɛdəˌɡoʊdʒi/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Meaning:

Pedagogy refers to the art or science of teaching and education. It includes teaching methods, strategies, and styles used by educators.

Example: The professor is an expert in modern pedagogy and uses innovative techniques in the classroom.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Teaching
  • Instruction
  • Education
  • Didactics
  • Training
  • Educational theory

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Let’s look at the origin (etymology) of the word "pedagogy".

📝It comes from Greek: paidagōgia

  • pais (paid-) = child
  • agōgos = leader or guide

✍️ Originally, the Greek word meant a slave who took children to school. Over time, it evolved to mean the methods and theory of teaching.

💡 In English, "pedagogy" now refers to the theory and practice of education and teaching methods.
