🔷 polygon /ˈpɑːlɪɡɑːn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A flat shape with three or more straight sides and angles, typically having five or more sides.
多边形；一种具有三条或更多直边和角的平面图形，通常指五边形以上。

Example: A triangle is the simplest kind of polygon.
例句：三角形是最简单的一种多边形。
Example: Architects often use polygons in modern building designs.
例句：建筑师经常在现代建筑设计中使用多边形。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Geometric figure 几何图形
  • Shape 形状
  • Plane figure 平面图形
  • Closed figure 封闭图形

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Polygon" comes from the Greek words poly- meaning “many” and gōnia meaning “angle.” It literally means “many angles.”
polygon 来自希腊语 poly-（多）和 gōnia（角），字面意思是“多角形”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: polygonos – many-angled
希腊语 polygonos：多角的
⬇️
Latin: polygonum – polygon
拉丁语 polygonum：多边形
⬇️
Modern English: polygon – shape with many sides
现代英语 polygon：多边形
