💊 pharmaceutical /ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkl/

Part of speech: Adjective / Noun
词性：形容词 / 名词

📖 Meaning:

As an adjective: Related to the production and sale of medicines.
形容词：与药物的制造与销售有关的。

As a noun (usually plural): A medicinal drug.
名词（通常为复数）：药品，药物。

Example: The pharmaceutical industry is heavily regulated.
例句：制药行业受到严格监管。
Example: New pharmaceuticals are being developed to fight cancer.
例句：新的药物正在研发中，用于对抗癌症。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Medicinal 药用的
  • Drug-related 与药物有关的
  • Therapeutic 治疗的
  • Medication 药物（名词）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Pharmaceutical” comes from Greek pharmakeutikos, meaning “of drugs,” from pharmakon, meaning “drug or medicine.”
“pharmaceutical” 来自希腊语 pharmakeutikos，意为“关于药物的”，词根 pharmakon 指“药物或毒药”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: pharmakon – drug, potion
希腊语 pharmakon：药物、药剂
⬇️
Late Latin: pharmaceuticus – of drugs
晚期拉丁语 pharmaceuticus：与药品有关的
⬇️
Modern English: pharmaceutical – related to drugs or medication
现代英语 pharmaceutical：与药品或医药行业有关
