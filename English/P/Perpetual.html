🌀 perpetual /pərˈpɛtʃuəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Perpetual means continuing forever or for a very long time without stopping; occurring repeatedly or constantly.
perpetual 的意思是“永久的，持续不断的”，也可以表示“反复发生的，没完没了的”。

Example: The region is in a state of perpetual conflict.
例句：这个地区处于持续冲突的状态中。
Example: She lived in a state of perpetual worry.
例句：她一直处于持续的焦虑之中。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Everlasting 永恒的
  • Unceasing 不停的
  • Endless 无尽的
  • Incessant 持续不断的
  • Constant 持续的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Perpetual" comes from the Latin word perpetuus, meaning “continuous, uninterrupted,” from per (through) + petere (to seek or aim at).
perpetual 源自拉丁语 perpetuus，意思是“持续不断的”，由 per（贯穿）+ petere（追求、寻求）组成。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: perpetuus – continuous, uninterrupted
拉丁语 perpetuus：持续不断的
⬇️
Old French: perpetuel – enduring, everlasting
古法语 perpetuel：持久的
⬇️
Modern English: perpetual – never-ending, constant
现代英语 perpetual：永恒的，持续不断的
