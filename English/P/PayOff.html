💸 pay off /peɪ ɔːf/

Part of speech: Phrasal Verb
词性：动词短语

📖 Meaning:

Pay off has two main meanings:

  1. To finish paying a debt or loan.
  2. To result in success or reward after effort.
pay off 有两个主要含义：1. 偿清债务；2. 努力有回报，取得成功。

Example: She finally paid off all her student loans.
例句：她终于还清了所有学生贷款。
Example: All those hours of practice really paid off when he won the competition.
例句：长时间的练习终于有了回报，他赢得了比赛。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Repay 偿还
  • Clear (a debt) 清偿（债务）
  • Be worth it 值得
  • Reap the rewards 收获回报
  • Come to fruition 取得成果

🔍 Etymology of the phrase /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Pay off" originated from the financial world, where it meant to fully pay a balance or obligation. Over time, it took on a figurative meaning—achieving success or reward.
“Pay off” 最初用于金融领域，表示“偿清欠款”，后来引申为“付出有回报、取得成果”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: pæġan – to pay
古英语 pæġan：支付
⬇️
Modern English: pay off – to finish paying OR to be successful
现代英语 pay off：偿清或取得成果

📊 Comparison
