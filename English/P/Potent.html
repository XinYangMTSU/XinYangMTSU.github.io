💪 potent /ˈpoʊtənt/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Having great power, influence, or effectiveness.
具有强大的力量、影响力或效果。

Example: The medicine is potent enough to relieve pain quickly.
例句：这种药效很强，能迅速止痛。
Example: He delivered a potent speech that moved everyone.
例句：他发表了一场充满影响力的演讲，打动了每个人。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Powerful 强大的
  • Strong 有力的
  • Effective 有效的
  • Influential 有影响力的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Potent" comes from Latin potens, meaning "powerful."
potent 来自拉丁语 potens，意为“强大的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: potens – powerful
拉丁语 potens：强大的
⬇️
Middle English: potent – influential, powerful
中古英语 potent：有影响力的，强大的
⬇️
Modern English: potent – powerful, effective
现代英语 potent：强大的，有效的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Powerful Neutral The engine is powerful enough to pull heavy loads. General
Effective Neutral / Formal Exercise is effective for maintaining health. Scientific / Practical
Potent Neutral / Formal They used potent arguments to convince the jury. Persuasive / authoritative
