💰 prosperous /ˈprɑːspərəs/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Successful, especially in terms of wealth, growth, or good fortune.
繁荣的；兴旺的；成功的（尤其指财富、发展或好运）。

Example: The country became more prosperous after the reforms.
例句：改革后，这个国家变得更加繁荣。
Example: She leads a prosperous life with a stable career and family.
例句：她过着事业稳定、家庭幸福的富足生活。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Wealthy 富有的
  • Successful 成功的
  • Thriving 兴旺的
  • Flourishing 繁荣的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Prosperous" comes from the Latin word prosperus, meaning “favorable” or “fortunate.” It entered English in the late Middle Ages with the sense of thriving or succeeding.
prosperous 源自拉丁语 prosperus，意为“有利的”或“幸运的”。在中世纪晚期进入英语，含义发展为“繁荣”或“成功”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: prosperus – favorable, fortunate
拉丁语 prosperus：顺利的，幸运的
⬇️
Middle English: prosperous – thriving, successful
中世纪英语 prosperous：兴旺的，成功的
⬇️
Modern English: prosperous – wealthy or thriving
现代英语 prosperous：富足的，繁荣的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Wealthy Neutral He grew up in a wealthy family. Factual
Thriving Positive The business is thriving in the new market. Energetic
Prosperous Formal The region became prosperous after trade expanded. Optimistic / Elevated
