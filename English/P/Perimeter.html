🌟 perimeter /pəˈrɪmɪtər/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Meaning:

The perimeter is the total length around the boundary of a two-dimensional shape. It is the distance one would travel by walking all the way around the shape.

Example: To find the perimeter of a rectangle, add up the lengths of all four sides. (= P = 2 × (length + width))

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Edge
  • Boundary
  • Outline
  • Border
  • Circumference (for circles)
  • Fringe

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word perimeter comes from the Greek word perimetros, meaning "measure around".

  • peri- = around
  • metron = measure

💡 So, the perimeter literally means “measuring around something.”
