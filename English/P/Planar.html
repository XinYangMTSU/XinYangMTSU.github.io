📐 planar /ˈpleɪnər/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Relating to or lying in a plane; flat or two-dimensional in form.
平面的；二维的；与平面有关的。

Example: The object was modeled using planar geometry.
例句：该物体是用平面几何建模的。
Example: The planar surface makes it easy to apply decals or textures.
例句：平坦的表面便于贴上图案或纹理。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Flat 平的
  • Two-dimensional 二维的
  • Level 平坦的
  • Surface-based 基于表面的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Planar" comes from the Latin word planus, meaning “flat” or “level.” It entered English via Late Latin as a technical or mathematical term.
planar 来自拉丁语 planus，意为“平的”、“水平的”，通过晚期拉丁语进入英语，常用于数学或技术语境。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: planus – flat, level
拉丁语 planus：平的，水平的
⬇️
Late Latin / Scientific English: planaris – of or relating to a plane
晚期拉丁语 planaris：与平面有关
⬇️
Modern English: planar – flat or two-dimensional
现代英语 planar：平面的，二维的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Flat Informal The floor is flat and smooth. Simple
Two-dimensional Technical A drawing is two-dimensional. Scientific
Planar Technical / Academic The planar structure is common in geometry. Precise
