🎥 pan out /pæn aʊt/

Part of speech: Phrasal Verb
词性：动词短语

📖 Meaning:

To turn out or result in a particular way, especially a successful one.
结果是……（尤指成功）；（事情）发展得……

Example: We’ll see how things pan out after the interview.
例句：面试之后我们看看事情会如何发展。
Example: His business idea didn’t pan out as he had hoped.
例句：他的商业想法并未如他所希望的那样成功。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Work out 顺利进行
  • Turn out 结果是
  • Play out 发展
  • Succeed (context-dependent) 成功（视上下文而定）

🔍 Etymology of the phrase /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Pan out" originally comes from gold panning — if gold appeared in the pan, the effort "panned out." Over time, it came to mean "turn out successfully."
“Pan out” 源自淘金术语 —— 如果淘到金子，就表示“成功”。后来引申为“有结果，成功”。

🧭 Phrase Evolution

Gold Rush Era: pan out – to find gold in a pan
淘金时期：pan out 表示在淘盘中发现金子
⬇️
Modern English: pan out – to result or develop successfully
现代英语：表示事情成功或有结果

📊 Comparison

Phrase Formality Level Example Use Tone
Work out Informal / Neutral Everything worked out in the end. Hopeful
Turn out Neutral It turned out better than expected. Descriptive
Pan out Informal / Idiomatic Let’s see how this pans out. Colloquial / Curious
