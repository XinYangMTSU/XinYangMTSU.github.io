⚖️ proportion /prəˈpɔːrʃn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Proportion refers to the relationship between two or more amounts, showing how much of one thing there is compared to another.
proportion 指两个或多个数量之间的比例关系，表示一件事物与另一件事物相比有多少。

Example: The proportion of boys to girls in the class is 3 to 2.
例句：班上的男女比例是 3 比 2。
Example: A small proportion of the students failed the test.
例句：只有少部分学生考试没及格。

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "proportion" comes from Latin proportio, meaning "relation or symmetry," based on pro (for) and portio (a part or share).
proportion 一词来自拉丁语 proportio，意为“关系”或“对称”，由 pro（为了）和 portio（部分）组成。

📊 Comparison

Term Usage Example Register

Percentage Part per hundred 60% of the team voted yes. Everyday
