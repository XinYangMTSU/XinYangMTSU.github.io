🖼️ photorealistic /ˌfəʊtəʊˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Photorealistic describes artwork, images, or graphics that are extremely detailed and realistic, almost indistinguishable from a real photograph.
“photorealistic” 形容的是极其逼真、细节丰富、几乎与真实照片难以区分的图像或艺术作品。

Example: The artist created a photorealistic portrait using only colored pencils.
例句：这位艺术家仅用彩铅创作了一幅逼真的人物肖像。
Example: Modern video games often feature photorealistic graphics.
例句：现代电子游戏通常具有逼真的图像效果。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Hyperrealistic 超写实的
  • Detailed 细节丰富的
  • Lifelike 栩栩如生的
  • True-to-life 逼真的
  • Naturalistic 自然主义风格的

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Photorealistic” combines the words photo (from Greek “phōs,” meaning “light”) and realistic (from Latin “res,” meaning “thing” or “reality”). It literally means “realistic as a photograph.”
“photorealistic” 是由 “photo”（光）与 “realistic”（现实的）组成，意为“如照片般真实”。

📊 Comparison with Related Words
