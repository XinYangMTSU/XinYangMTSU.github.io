🎭 protagonist /prəˈtæɡənɪst/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A protagonist is the **main character** in a story, play, or movie—the person the story is centered around.
It can also refer to a **leading figure** in a real situation or movement.
“protagonist” 指的是小说、戏剧或电影中的主角，也可以指现实中的领导者或积极参与者。

Example: Harry Potter is the protagonist of the famous book series.
例句：哈利·波特是这套著名小说的主角。
Example: She was a key protagonist in the campaign for education reform.
例句：她是教育改革运动中的主要推动者之一。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Hero 英雄 / 主角
  • Main character 主要角色
  • Leading figure 领导人物
  • Central character 核心角色
  • Champion 拥护者 / 倡导者

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Protagonist” comes from Greek protagonistes, where proto- means “first” and agonistes means “actor in a struggle.”
“protagonist” 来自希腊语 protagonistēs：其中 proto- 表示“第一”，agonistes 表示“参赛者、演员”。
