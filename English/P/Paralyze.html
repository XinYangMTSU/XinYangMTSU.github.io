🧊 paralyze /ˈpærəˌlaɪz/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To paralyze means to cause someone to lose the ability to move or to make something unable to function properly.
paralyze 的意思是使某人失去行动能力，或使某物无法正常运作。

Example: The accident paralyzed him from the waist down.
例句：这次事故使他下半身瘫痪。
Example: The cyberattack paralyzed the entire network for hours.
例句：网络攻击使整个网络瘫痪了好几个小时。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Disable 使残疾，使无法运作
  • Immobilize 使无法移动
  • Freeze 冻结
  • Cripple 削弱，瘫痪
  • Shut down 关闭，使停顿

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Paralyze" comes from the Greek paralusis, meaning "relaxation or loosening," from para (beside) and lysis (loosening). It entered English through Latin and French.
paralyze 来自希腊语 paralusis，意为“松弛、无力”，由 para（在旁边）和 lysis（松开）组成，经拉丁语和法语传入英语。
