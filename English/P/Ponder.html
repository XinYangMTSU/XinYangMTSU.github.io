🤔 ponder /ˈpɑːndər/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

To ponder means to think deeply or carefully about something, especially before making a decision or reaching a conclusion.
ponder 意为“沉思、深思熟虑”，尤指在做出决定或结论前的深入思考。

Example: She sat by the window, pondering the meaning of life.
例句：她坐在窗边，沉思人生的意义。
Example: He pondered whether to accept the job offer or not.
例句：他思考是否要接受那份工作邀请。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Contemplate 沉思
  • Reflect 反思
  • Consider 考虑
  • Deliberate 深思熟虑
  • Muse 冥想

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Ponder" comes from Latin ponderare, meaning "to weigh" or "consider carefully," from pondus (weight).
ponder 源自拉丁语 ponderare，意为“称重、仔细考虑”，来自 pondus（重量）。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: pondus / ponderare – weight, to weigh or reflect
拉丁语 pondus / ponderare：重量，衡量或思考
⬇️
Old French: ponderer – to weigh mentally
古法语 ponderer：心理上的权衡
⬇️
Modern English: ponder – to think deeply about something
现代英语 ponder：深入思考

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
