🧑‍🎨 poet /ˈpəʊɪt/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Poet is a person who writes poems or creates poetry.
“poet” 是指写诗或创作诗歌的人。

Example: Maya Angelou was a famous American poet.
例句：玛雅·安杰卢是一位著名的美国诗人。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Verse writer 诗歌作者
  • Bard 吟游诗人
  • Lyricist 歌词作者
  • Wordsmith 文字匠人
  • Writer 作家

✨ poetic /pəʊˈɛtɪk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Poetic describes something that is beautiful, expressive, or characteristic of poetry.
“poetic” 描述某物美丽、有表现力，或具有诗意特征。

Example: Her speech was so poetic that it moved the entire audience.
例句：她的演讲充满诗意，感动了在场的每一个人。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Lyrical 抒情的
  • Artistic 艺术的
  • Romantic 浪漫的
  • Expressive 富有表现力的
  • Imaginative 富于想象的

📝 poetry /ˈpəʊətri/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Poetry is a form of literary expression that uses rhythm, imagery, and metaphor to evoke emotions and ideas.
“poetry” 是一种使用节奏、意象和比喻来表达情感和思想的文学形式。

Example: He enjoys reading poetry before bed.
例句：他喜欢在睡前读诗。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Verse 诗句
  • Rhyme 押韵诗
  • Lyric 抒情诗
  • Ode 颂诗
  • Ballad 叙事诗

🔍 Etymology

📚 All three words originate from the Greek word poiein, meaning “to make.”
这三个词都源自希腊语 poiein，意为“创造”或“做”。
