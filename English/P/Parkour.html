🤸 parkour /pɑːrˈkʊr/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A sport or physical discipline in which people move through urban environments by running, jumping, and climbing over obstacles in the most efficient way possible.
一种运动形式，通过奔跑、跳跃和攀爬，尽可能高效地穿越城市障碍。

Example: He practices parkour by jumping between rooftops and climbing walls.
例句：他通过在屋顶间跳跃和攀爬墙壁练习跑酷。
Example: Parkour athletes train to move with agility, speed, and control.
例句：跑酷运动员训练自己具备灵活性、速度和控制力。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Freerunning 自由奔跑
  • Urban acrobatics 城市杂技
  • Obstacle running 障碍跑
  • Movement training 动作训练

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Parkour" comes from the French word parcours, meaning "course" or "route", and was popularized by David Belle in the late 20th century.
parkour 源自法语 parcours，意为“路线”或“路径”，由大卫·贝尔于 20 世纪末推广。

🧭 Word Evolution

French: parcours – route, journey
法语 parcours：路线，旅程
⬇️
Modern Use: parkour – efficient movement over obstacles
现代用法 parkour：高效越过障碍的运动形式
