🔗 association /əˌsoʊ.siˈeɪ.ʃən/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Association means a connection or relationship between people, things, or ideas. It can also refer to a group or organization formed for a common purpose.
association 可以表示事物之间的“联系”或“联想”，也可以指“协会、团体”等组织。

Example: I always have a strong association between summer and ice cream.
例句：夏天和冰淇淋之间，我总有很强的联想。
Example: She joined the National Teachers' Association last year.
例句：她去年加入了国家教师协会。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Connection 联系
  • Link 关联
  • Relation 关系
  • Affiliation 隶属
  • Organization 组织

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Association" comes from Latin associare, meaning "to unite with," from ad- (to) + sociare (to join or ally).
association 源自拉丁语 associare，意思是“与……结合”，由 ad-（向）和 sociare（结合、结盟）组成。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: associare – to unite or join
拉丁语 associare：结合、联合
⬇️
French: association – act of joining or connecting
法语 association：联合、联系
⬇️
English: association – relationship or organized group
英语 association：联想、关系、协会

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Link Neutral There’s a clear link between stress and sleep problems. Descriptive
Affiliation Formal Their political affiliation is unclear. Institutional
Association Neutral / Academic The color red has strong cultural associations. Conceptual
