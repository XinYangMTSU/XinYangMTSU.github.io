🌟 adhere /ədˈhɪr/

Part of speech: Verb

📖 Definition:

Adhere means to stick firmly to a surface or to follow a set of rules or beliefs.

Example: Please make sure the label adheres properly to the package.
Example: We must adhere to the safety regulations at all times.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Stick
  • Cling
  • Comply
  • Follow
  • Observe
  • Abide by

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Let’s look at the origin (etymology) of the word "adhere".

📝It comes from Latin adhaerēre, meaning "to stick to" — from ad- (to) + haerēre (to stick).
