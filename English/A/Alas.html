😔 alas /əˈlæs/

Part of speech: Interjection
词性：感叹词

📖 Meaning:

An expression of sorrow, grief, or regret. Often used in literature or storytelling to show sadness or disappointment.
表示悲伤、遗憾或惋惜的感叹词，常用于文学或叙述中。

Example: Alas, the hero arrived too late to save the kingdom.
例句：唉，英雄来得太晚，未能拯救王国。
Example: She had hoped for a letter, but alas, none came.
例句：她原本盼望能收到一封信，但可惜没有。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Oh no 哎呀
  • Woe 哀叹
  • Sadly 悲伤地
  • Regrettably 遗憾地

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Alas" comes from Middle English, likely a combination of "ah" (a cry) and "las" (loss), expressing a feeling of sorrow or misfortune.
“Alas” 源自中古英语，可能由 “ah”（哀叹）与 “las”（损失）组合而成，用于表达悲伤或不幸之情。

🧭 Word Evolution

Middle English: alas – expression of grief
中古英语 alas：悲伤的感叹词

Old French: hélas – oh, woe!
古法语 hélas：哎呀，可惜

Modern English: alas – sorrowful expression
现代英语 alas：表达悲伤或遗憾

📊 Comparison

Word Context Example Use Tone
