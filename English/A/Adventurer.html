🧭 adventurer /ədˈven.tʃər.ər/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

An adventurer is a person who seeks out exciting, risky, or unusual experiences—often involving travel, exploration, or bold action.
“adventurer” 指的是一个追求刺激、冒险或非凡经历的人，通常与旅行、探索或大胆行为有关。

Example: The adventurer set off to explore the Amazon rainforest.
例句：那位冒险者踏上了探索亚马逊雨林的旅程。
Example: She lived like a true adventurer, always chasing the unknown.
例句：她活得像个真正的冒险家，总是追逐未知。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Explorer 探险者
  • Risk-taker 冒险者
  • Voyager 航行者
  • Daredevil 胆大妄为的人
  • Wanderer 流浪者

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Adventurer" comes from the Old French aventureor, which traces back to Latin adventura, meaning "what is about to happen"—from advenire, "to arrive".
“adventurer” 源自古法语 aventureor，追溯至拉丁语 adventura，意为“即将发生的事”，来自 advenire，意为“到来”。
