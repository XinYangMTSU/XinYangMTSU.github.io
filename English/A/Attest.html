🌟 attest /əˈtɛst/

Part of speech: Verb

📖 As a verb (action):

To attest means to show, prove, or declare that something is true, often based on personal knowledge or evidence.

Example: Many documents attest to her long career in the public service. (= They serve as proof.)

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Confirm
  • Verify
  • Affirm
  • Prove
  • Substantiate
  • Bear witness

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word attest comes from the Latin verb attestari, meaning “to bear witness to.”

  • ad- = to or toward
  • testari = to testify, give evidence

💡 Originally used in legal and religious contexts to refer to giving formal testimony or witnessing truth.
