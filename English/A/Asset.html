💎 asset /ˈæset/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Something valuable or useful that a person or organization owns, has, or benefits from; a positive quality or resource.
资产；有价值的人或物；优点。

Example: Her communication skills are a great asset to the team.
例句：她的沟通能力是团队的重要资产。
Example: The company listed its assets and liabilities in the report.
例句：公司在报告中列出了其资产与负债。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Advantage 优势
  • Resource 资源
  • Benefit 益处
  • Strength 优点；长处

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Asset" comes from the Anglo-Norman legal phrase asetz, meaning “enough,” derived from Latin ad satis ("to enough"), referring to having enough property to pay debts.
“Asset” 源自英诺曼法语 asetz，意为“足够”，来自拉丁语 ad satis（足够），原意为“拥有足够的财产来偿还债务”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: ad satis – to enough
拉丁语 ad satis：足够

Old French: asez – enough, sufficient
古法语 assez：足够的

Modern English: asset – valuable item or quality
现代英语 asset：有价值的事物或优点
