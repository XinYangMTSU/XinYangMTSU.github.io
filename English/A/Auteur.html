🎬 auteur /oʊˈtɜːr/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

An auteur is a filmmaker, usually a director, who has a distinct personal style and complete control over all aspects of a film's production.
“auteur” 指的是一位具有鲜明个人风格，并对电影制作拥有全面掌控的导演或电影创作者。

Example: Stanley Kubrick is widely considered an auteur because of his unique vision and control over his films.
例句：斯坦利·库布里克被广泛认为是电影作者，因为他对作品具有独特视角和全面控制。
Example: The film was praised for its auteur-driven direction and artistic choices.
例句：这部电影因其“作者式”导演风格和艺术选择而受到好评。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Visionary 有远见的人
  • Director 导演
  • Creator 创作者
  • Filmmaker 电影制作者
  • Artist 艺术家

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Auteur” comes directly from the French word for “author.” It was adopted in English especially in the context of film criticism to describe directors who leave a strong personal stamp on their work.
“auteur” 源自法语，意为“作者”。该词被引入英语用于电影评论，特指那些在电影中展现强烈个人风格的导演。
