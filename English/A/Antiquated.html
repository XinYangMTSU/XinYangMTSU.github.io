🕰️ antiquated /ˈæn.tɪ.kweɪ.tɪd/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Antiquated describes something that is old-fashioned, outdated, or no longer useful due to being from an earlier time.
antiquated 的意思是“过时的、陈旧的、不再适用的”，通常指来自较早时期的事物。

Example: That antiquated computer system needs to be replaced.
例句：那套陈旧的计算机系统需要更换了。
Example: His views on gender roles seem antiquated.
例句：他对性别角色的看法似乎已经过时了。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Outdated 过时的
  • Obsolete 废弃的
  • Old-fashioned 老式的
  • Archaic 古老的
  • Vintage (context-dependent) 复古的（视语境而定）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Antiquated" comes from the Latin root antiquatus, past participle of antiquare, meaning “to make old,” from antiquus (ancient, old).
antiquated 源自拉丁语 antiquatus，为 antiquare（使变旧）的过去分词，来自 antiquus，意为“古老的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: antiquatus – made old
拉丁语 antiquatus：变旧的
⬇️
Old French: antiqué – antiquated
古法语 antiqué：陈旧的
⬇️
Modern English: antiquated – outdated, no longer used
现代英语 antiquated：过时的，不再使用的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Obsolete Formal The machine is now obsolete. Neutral / Technical
Old-fashioned Informal He wears old-fashioned clothes. Neutral / Descriptive
