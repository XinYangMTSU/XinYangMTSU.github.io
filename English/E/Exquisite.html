💎 exquisite /ˈɛks.kwɪ.zɪt/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Exquisite means extremely beautiful and delicate, or exceptionally fine and pleasing in detail or quality.
“exquisite” 意为极其精美、精致或出类拔萃，常用于形容艺术品、感受或品质。

Example: The artist's work is known for its exquisite detail.
例句：这位艺术家的作品以其精致的细节而闻名。
Example: She wore an exquisite silk dress to the gala.
例句：她穿着一件精美的丝绸礼服参加晚会。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Elegant 优雅的
  • Delicate 精致的
  • Refined 精炼的
  • Beautiful 美丽的
  • Superb 极佳的

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Exquisite" comes from Latin *exquisitus*, meaning "carefully sought out," from *exquirere* (to search out), combining *ex-* (out) and *quaerere* (to seek).
“exquisite” 源自拉丁语 *exquisitus*，意为“精心挑选的”，由 *ex-*（出）和 *quaerere*（寻找）组成。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
