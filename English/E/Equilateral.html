🔺 equilateral /ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Having all sides of equal length.
等边的；所有边长相等的。

Example: An equilateral triangle has three equal sides and angles.
例句：等边三角形有三条相等的边和角。
Example: The design was based on equilateral shapes for balance and symmetry.
例句：该设计基于等边图形，以获得平衡与对称。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Regular 规则的（几何）
  • Symmetrical 对称的
  • Even-sided 边长均等的
  • Uniform 统一的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Equilateral" comes from the Latin roots aequus (equal) and lateralis (side). It literally means “equal-sided.”
equilateral 来自拉丁语 aequus（相等）和 lateralis（边），意为“边长相等的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

拉丁语 aequilateralis：边长相等的

⬇️
