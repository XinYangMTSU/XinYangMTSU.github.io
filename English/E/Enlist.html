🫡 enlist /ɪnˈlɪst/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Enlist has two common meanings:

  1. To formally join the military or armed services.
  2. To ask for or gain support, help, or participation in a cause or activity.
enlist 有两个常见含义：
1. 正式加入军队；
2. 请求或获得某人的帮助或参与某项事业。

Example: He decided to enlist in the army after finishing college.
例句：他大学毕业后决定参军。
Example: The project manager enlisted the help of several experts.
例句：项目经理征求了几位专家的帮助。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Recruit 招募
  • Join 加入
  • Engage 动员
  • Sign up 报名
  • Mobilize 调动

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Enlist" comes from the French word enlister, meaning “to enroll” or “to register.” It originally referred to writing someone's name on a list of soldiers or participants.
“enlist” 源自法语 enlister，意为“登记、注册”，最初用于把某人列入士兵或参与者名单。

🧭 Word Evolution

French: enlister – to register on a list
法语 enlister：登记在名单上
⬇️
Modern English: enlist – to join a cause or ask for help
现代英语 enlist：加入某事业，或请求帮助

📊 Comparison
