↔️ lateral /ˈlætərəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Relating to or situated at the side; moving or directed sideways rather than up or down.
侧面的；横向的；向旁边的。

Example: The lateral movement of the car made it hard to park.
例句：汽车的横向移动让停车变得困难。
Example: Promotions in the company are not always upward — sometimes there are lateral moves to different departments.
例句：公司中的晋升并不总是向上，有时是横向调动到其他部门。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Sideways 向侧面地
  • Horizontal 水平的
  • Peripheral 外围的
  • Oblique 斜向的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Lateral" comes from Latin lateralis, derived from latus, meaning “side.”
lateral 源自拉丁语 lateralis，来自 latus，意为“侧面”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: latus – side
拉丁语 latus：侧面
⬇️
Late Latin: lateralis – relating to the side
晚期拉丁语 lateralis：与侧面相关
⬇️
Modern English: lateral – side or sideways
现代英语 lateral：横向的，侧面的
