💡 lucid /ˈluːsɪd/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Expressed clearly; easy to understand.
表达清晰的；容易理解的。

Example: She gave a lucid explanation of the complex theory.
例句：她对这个复杂理论进行了清晰的解释。
Example: His writing is always lucid and logical.
例句：他的写作总是清晰且有逻辑。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Clear 清晰的
  • Understandable 易懂的
  • Coherent 连贯的
  • Articulate 表达清楚的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Lucid" comes from Latin lucidus, meaning "light" or "clear," from lux meaning "light."
lucid 源自拉丁语 lucidus，意思是“明亮”或“清晰”，源自 lux（光）。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: lucidus – bright, clear
拉丁语 lucidus：明亮，清晰
⬇️
Modern English: lucid – clearly expressed or easy to understand
现代英语 lucid：表达清晰或易于理解的

📊 Comparison
