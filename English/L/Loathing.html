😖 loathing /ˈloʊðɪŋ/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Loathing means a strong feeling of hatred or disgust for someone or something.
loathing 的意思是对某人或某事强烈的厌恶或憎恨。

Example: She felt nothing but loathing for the corrupt politician.
例句：她对那个腐败的政客只有厌恶。
Example: His loathing of violence was evident in his writing.
例句：他对暴力的憎恨在他的作品中显而易见。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Hatred 仇恨
  • Disgust 厌恶
  • Aversion 反感
  • Detestation 憎恶
  • Repulsion 厌恶感
