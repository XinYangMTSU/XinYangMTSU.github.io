📚 literary /ˈlɪt̬.ə.rer.i/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Literary means related to literature or writing that has artistic value (like novels, poetry, essays). It can also describe someone involved in studying or writing literature.
literary 表示与文学、写作有关的，尤其是具有艺术价值的写作（如小说、诗歌、散文）；也可以形容某人从事文学研究或创作。

Example: She’s part of a literary club that meets every Saturday.
例句：她是一个每周六聚会的文学俱乐部成员。
Example: His writing has a very literary style.
例句：他的写作风格非常文学化。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Bookish 书卷气的
  • Artistic 有艺术性的
  • Scholarly 学术性的
  • Poetic 富有诗意的
  • Refined 文雅的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "literary" comes from the Latin word litterarius, meaning “relating to letters or learning.”
“literary”一词来自拉丁语 litterarius，意思是“与文字或学问有关的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: litterarius – of letters, learned
拉丁语 litterarius：文字的、有学问的
⬇️
Old French: litteraire
古法语 litteraire
⬇️
English: literary /ˈlɪt̬.ə.rer.i/
英语：literary，现代英语中用于表示文学相关
