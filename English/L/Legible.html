🌟 legible /ˈlɛdʒ.ə.bəl/

Part of speech: Adjective

📖 Meaning:

Legible means clear enough to read. It usually describes handwriting or printed text that can be read without difficulty.

Example: The doctor’s handwriting was surprisingly legible.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Readable
  • Clear
  • Understandable
  • Neat
  • Decipherable
  • Intelligible

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Let’s look at the origin of the word "legible".

📝 It comes from the Latin verb legere, which means "to read."

  • legere = to read

✍️ Over time, it evolved through Late Latin legibilis into French lisible and then into English as legible.

🧭 Word Evolution
