⏳ lapsed /læpst/

Part of speech: Verb / Adjective (past tense and past participle of “lapse”)
词性：动词 / 形容词（“lapse”的过去式和过去分词）

📖 Meaning:

No longer valid, active, or following something once practiced or observed.
失效的；终止的；放弃了（某种行为、信仰或习惯）。

Example: His membership has lapsed due to non-payment.
例句：由于未缴费，他的会员资格已失效。
Example: She’s a lapsed Catholic who no longer attends church.
例句：她是一位不再去教堂的前天主教徒。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Expired 过期的
  • Terminated 终止的
  • Abandoned 放弃的
  • Inactive 不活跃的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Lapsed" comes from the Latin word lapsus, meaning “a slip” or “fall,” from the verb labi, meaning “to slide.”
lapsed 源自拉丁语 lapsus，意为“滑倒”或“失误”，来自动词 labi，意为“滑动”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: lapsus – slip, fall
拉丁语 lapsus：失误，滑倒
⬇️
Middle English: lapsen – to fall away
中古英语 lapsen：衰退，堕落
⬇️
Modern English: lapsed – no longer active or valid
现代英语 lapsed：失效的、不再遵守的
