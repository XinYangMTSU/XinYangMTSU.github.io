🌱 origin /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪn/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The point or place where something begins, arises, or is derived.
起源；起点；来源。

Example: The origin of the universe remains one of science’s greatest mysteries.
例句：宇宙的起源仍是科学最大的谜题之一。
Example: She’s proud of her family’s cultural origin.
例句：她为自己家族的文化起源感到自豪。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Beginning 开始
  • Source 来源
  • Root 根源
  • Genesis 起源；创始

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Origin" comes from the Latin word origo, meaning “a beginning, source, birth.” It stems from the verb oriri, “to rise or emerge.”
origin 来自拉丁语 origo，意为“开始、来源、出生”，源自动词 oriri，意为“升起、出现”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: oriri – to rise, emerge
拉丁语 oriri：升起，出现
⬇️
Latin: origo – beginning, source
拉丁语 origo：起源，开始
⬇️
Modern English: origin – point where something begins
现代英语 origin：起源、开始之处
