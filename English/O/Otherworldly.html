🌌 otherworldly /ˌʌðəˈwɜːldli/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Strange, mysterious, or beautiful in a way that seems not to belong to this world; relating to a spiritual or imaginary realm beyond everyday life.
超凡脱俗的；似乎不属于这个世界的；神秘而奇异的。

Example: The aurora borealis has an otherworldly beauty.
例句：极光拥有一种超凡脱俗的美感。
Example: Her singing had an otherworldly quality that moved the entire audience.
例句：她的歌声有一种不属于尘世的气质，打动了全场听众。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Ethereal 飘渺的；轻灵的
  • Unearthly 不可思议的；不属于尘世的
  • Supernatural 超自然的
  • Spiritual 精神上的；心灵的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Otherworldly" is a compound of "other" + "worldly". It literally means “from another world.” It evolved to describe things that seem outside our normal, earthly experience.
“Otherworldly” 是“other（另一个）”和“worldly（尘世的）”的组合，字面意思是“来自另一个世界的”。后来引申为“非现实、超自然、神秘”的感觉。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: other + world (weoruld) + -ly suffix
古英语：other（另一个）+ world（世界）+ -ly（形容词后缀）

Modern English: otherworldly – beyond earthly or material reality
现代英语：otherworldly 表示“非现实的，脱俗的，神秘的”
