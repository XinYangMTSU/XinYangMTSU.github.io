🌱 thin out /θɪn aʊt/

Part of speech: Phrasal Verb
词性：短语动词

📖 Meaning:

Thin out means to become or make something less dense, crowded, or thick—either physically or metaphorically.
“thin out” 的意思是变得稀疏或让某物变得不那么密集，可以是实际的（如植物、人群）或比喻的（如头发、感情）。

Example: As the children grew older, their baby fat thinned out.
例句：随着孩子们长大，他们的婴儿肥慢慢变得稀薄了。
Example: The crowd began to thin out after the concert ended.
例句：音乐会结束后，人群开始散去。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Shrink 缩小
  • Reduce 减少
  • Disperse 分散
  • Fade 逐渐减弱
  • Lighten 变稀薄

🔍 Etymology of the phrase /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 “Thin out” comes from the adjective “thin” (not thick or dense) combined with the particle “out,” implying reduction or spreading away from a center.
“thin out” 来自形容词 thin（稀薄的）加上副词 out，表示“向外扩散”或“减少密度”。
