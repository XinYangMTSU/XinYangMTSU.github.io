🌟 transformative /trænsˈfɔːrmətɪv/

Part of speech: Adjective

📖 Definition:

Something that is transformative causes a major change or shift in someone or something, often improving it significantly.

Example: The invention of the internet was a transformative moment in human history.

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Revolutionary
  • Groundbreaking
  • Life-changing
  • Radical
  • Innovative
  • Disruptive

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word transformative comes from the base word transform, which originates from Latin:

  • trans- = across, beyond
  • formare = to form or shape

💡 So "transformative" literally means "able to change the shape or form of something across boundaries."
