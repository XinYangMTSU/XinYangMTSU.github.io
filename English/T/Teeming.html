🐟 teeming /ˈtiːmɪŋ/

Part of speech: Adjective / Present participle of verb "teem"
词性：形容词 / 动词“teem”的现在分词

📖 Meaning:

Full of life, people, animals, or activity; overflowing or swarming with something.
充满的；挤满的；到处都是的。

Example: The market was teeming with shoppers before the festival.
例句：节日前市场挤满了购物者。
Example: The rainforest is teeming with wildlife.
例句：热带雨林中生机勃勃，野生动物众多。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Overflowing 溢出的
  • Brimming 充满的
  • Swarming 蜂拥的
  • Alive with 充满……的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Teeming" comes from the Old English word tēman, meaning “to bring forth” or “to produce,” which evolved into the idea of abundance or overflowing.
teeming 来自古英语 tēman，意为“产生”或“繁殖”，后发展为“充满”或“溢出”的意思。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: tēman – to give birth, bring forth
古英语 tēman：产生、繁殖
⬇️
Middle English: temen – to abound
中古英语 temen：丰富、充满
⬇️
Modern English: teeming – full or swarming with
现代英语 teeming：充满……的，遍布……的

📊 Comparison
