🌟 triumph /ˈtraɪ.əmf/

Part of speech: Noun or verb

📖 As a verb (action):

To triumph means to achieve victory or success, especially after a struggle or challenge.

Example: The team triumphed over their rivals in the final game.

📖 As a noun (a thing):

A triumph is a great victory, achievement, or success.

Example: Winning the award was a triumph for her hard work and dedication.

🧬 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Victory
  • Success
  • Win
  • Achievement
  • Glory
  • Conquest

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "triumph" comes from the Latin word triumphus, meaning a ceremonial procession celebrating a military victory.

🔹 It passed through Old French triumphe into Middle English with its modern meaning of great success or victory.
