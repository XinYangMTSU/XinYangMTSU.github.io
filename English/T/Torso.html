🧍 torso /ˈtɔːrsoʊ/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The main part of the human body without the head, arms, or legs; the trunk.
人体的躯干部分，不包括头部、四肢；身体主干。

Example: He suffered injuries to his torso during the accident.
例句：事故中他躯干受伤。
Example: The sculpture displayed only the torso of the athlete.
例句：这尊雕像只展现了运动员的躯干。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Trunk 躯干
  • Body 身体
  • Core 核心（身体中部）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Torso" comes from Italian, originally meaning "stalk" or "trunk of a body." It refers to the central part of the human figure without limbs or head.
“Torso” 源自意大利语，原意为“茎干”或“身体主干”，用来表示没有四肢和头的中段身体部分。

🧭 Word Evolution

Italian: torso – trunk, stalk
意大利语 torso：树干、躯干

Latin root: thyrsus – stalk
拉丁词根 thyrsus：茎

Modern English: torso – body without limbs or head
现代英语 torso：无四肢和头的身体
