🔷 trapezoid /ˈtræpəˌzɔɪd/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A trapezoid is a flat shape with four sides, where only one pair of opposite sides is parallel.
trapezoid（梯形）是一种平面图形，有四条边，其中只有一对对边是平行的。

Example: In geometry class, we learned to calculate the area of a trapezoid.
例句：在几何课上，我们学习了如何计算梯形的面积。
Example: The table was shaped like a trapezoid to fit into the corner.
例句：这张桌子是梯形的，用来贴合角落的空间。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Quadrilateral 四边形
  • Trapezium (British English) 梯形（英式英语）
  • Four-sided shape 四边图形

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Trapezoid" comes from the Greek word trapeza meaning "table," from tra- ("four") and peza ("foot"). The shape was named for its resemblance to a table with uneven legs.
trapezoid 源自希腊语 trapeza，意思是“桌子”，由 tra-（四）和 peza（脚）组成，因为这种图形像一张腿长不等的桌子。

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: trapeza – table
希腊语 trapeza：桌子
⬇️
Late Latin: trapezium
拉丁语 trapezium：梯形
⬇️
Modern English: trapezoid – a four-sided shape with one pair of parallel sides
现代英语 trapezoid：有一对平行边的四边形
