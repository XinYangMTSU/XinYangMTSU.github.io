🌟 technicality /ˌtɛknɪˈkæləti/

Part of speech: Noun

📖 Definition:

A technicality is a small detail in a rule, law, or agreement, especially one that is important in affecting how something is done or whether something is legal or valid.

Example: The case was dismissed on a technicality. (= because of a small legal detail)

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Detail
  • Clause
  • Provision
  • Fine point
  • Loophole
  • Specification

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word "technicality" comes from technical + -ity, which turns an adjective into a noun.

📝 It developed in the 19th century to refer to details in specialized fields, particularly law and regulation.

🧭 Word Evolution

Greek: tekhnē (meaning art or skill) → English: technical + -ity → technicality
