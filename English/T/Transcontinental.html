🌍 transcontinental /ˌtrænzkɒntɪˈnentl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Spanning or relating to more than one continent, especially crossing an entire continent.
跨越或涉及一个以上大陆的；尤指横跨整个大陆的。

Example: The transcontinental railroad connected the East and West coasts of the U.S.
例句：横贯大陆铁路连接了美国的东海岸和西海岸。
Example: She booked a transcontinental flight from Paris to Tokyo.
例句：她订了一趟从巴黎飞往东京的洲际航班。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Intercontinental 洲际的
  • Global 全球的
  • Worldwide 全世界的
  • Cross-continental 跨大陆的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Transcontinental" is formed from Latin roots: trans- meaning “across” and continent meaning “landmass.”
transcontinental 源自拉丁语，trans- 意为“跨越”，continent 意为“大陆”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: trans – across, beyond
拉丁语 trans：穿越，超越
⬇️
English: continent – large landmass
英语 continent：大陆
⬇️
Modern English: transcontinental – across a continent
现代英语 transcontinental：横跨大陆的

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone

Global Neutral We face global environmental challenges. Broad / Informative
