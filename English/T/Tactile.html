✋ tactile /ˈtæktaɪl/ or /ˈtæktəl/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Tactile means related to the sense of touch. It can refer to anything you can physically feel or textures that are designed to be felt.
tactile 意为“触觉的；可以触摸到的”，用于描述与接触、触感有关的事物或体验。

Example: The fabric has a pleasant tactile quality that makes it comfortable to wear.
例句：这种布料有令人愉悦的触感，穿起来很舒服。
Example: The sculpture invites a tactile exploration with its rough and smooth surfaces.
例句：这座雕塑通过粗糙和光滑的表面引导观众去用手触摸体验。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Touchable 可触摸的
  • Palpable 可感知的
  • Physical 身体上的
  • Textured 有纹理的
  • Sensory 感官的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Tactile" comes from the Latin word tactilis, from tangere, meaning “to touch.” The word has evolved to describe anything connected with or sensed through touch.
“tactile” 来自拉丁语 tactilis，源自动词 tangere，意为“触碰”。现在用于描述一切与触觉或接触相关的事物。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: tangere – to touch
拉丁语 tangere：触摸
⬇️
Latin adjective: tactilis – able to be touched
拉丁形容词 tactilis：可以触碰的
⬇️
Modern English: tactile – relating to the sense of touch
现代英语 tactile：与触觉有关
