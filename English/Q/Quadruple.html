✖️ quadruple /kwɒˈdruːpl/

Part of speech: Verb / Adjective
词性：动词 / 形容词

📖 Meaning:

(v.) To increase or make four times as much or as many.
(adj.) Fourfold; consisting of four parts.
(动词) 增加到四倍；使成四倍。
(形容词) 四倍的；由四部分组成的。

Example: The company’s profits quadrupled in just two years.
例句：该公司的利润在短短两年内增长了四倍。
Example: He performed a quadruple jump during the competition.
例句：他在比赛中完成了一个四周跳。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Increase fourfold 增加四倍
  • Multiply by four 乘以四
  • Expand 扩展
  • Augment significantly 大幅增强

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Quadruple" comes from Latin quadruplus, from quattuor (four) + plus (fold). It entered English through Late Middle French.
“Quadruple” 源自拉丁语 quadruplus，由 quattuor（四）和 plus（倍）构成，经中古法语传入英语。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: quadruplus – fourfold
拉丁语 quadruplus：四倍

Old French: quadruple
古法语 quadruple

Modern English: quadruple – to multiply by four
现代英语 quadruple：乘以四倍
