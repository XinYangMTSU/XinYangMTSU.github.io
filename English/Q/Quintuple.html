✋ quintuple /kwɪnˈtuːpəl/ or /ˈkwɪntəpəl/

Part of speech: Verb / Adjective / Noun
词性：动词 / 形容词 / 名词

📖 Meaning:

As a verb: To increase something fivefold (by five times).
（动词）使变为五倍；增加到五倍。

As an adjective: Five times as much or as many.
（形容词）五倍的。

As a noun: A set or group of five things.
（名词）五个一组；五倍的量。

Example (verb): The company’s profits quintupled in just three years.
例句（动词）：公司的利润在短短三年内增长了五倍。
Example (adjective): They received quintuple the normal amount of entries this year.
例句（形容词）：他们今年收到的参赛作品是往年的五倍。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Multiply by five 乘以五
  • Increase fivefold 增加五倍
  • Five times 五倍
  • Expand drastically 大幅扩张

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Quintuple" comes from Latin quintus meaning “fifth” and plus meaning “more.” The word has been used in English since the 15th century to mean “fivefold.”
“Quintuple” 源自拉丁语 quintus（第五）和 plus（更多），自15世纪以来在英语中用于表示“五倍的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: quintus + plus – fifth + more
拉丁语 quintus（第五）+ plus（更多）

Middle English: quintuple – fivefold
中古英语 quintuple：五倍

Modern English: quintuple – multiply by five
现代英语 quintuple：乘以五，五倍
