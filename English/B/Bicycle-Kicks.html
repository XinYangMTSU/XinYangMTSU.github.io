🌟 bicycle kick /ˈbaɪsɪkl kɪk/

Part of speech: Noun (sports term)

📖 Definition:

A bicycle kick is a spectacular move in soccer where a player jumps into the air, leans backwards, and kicks the ball over their own head while in midair—resembling a pedaling motion.

Example: The striker scored an incredible goal with a bicycle kick in the final minutes.

📸 A soccer player performing a bicycle kick

🔍 Etymology and Origin

The term bicycle kick comes from the motion of the legs during the move, which resembles the pedaling of a bicycle. In some parts of the world, it’s also known as an overhead kick or scissors kick.

🧩 Synonyms

  • Overhead kick
  • Scissors kick
  • Reverse volley
