🧵 bespoke /bɪˈspoʊk/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Made specifically for a particular person; custom-made.
定做的；为特定客户量身定制的。

Example: He wore a bespoke suit tailored in Italy.
例句：他穿着一套意大利定制的西装。
Example: The company offers bespoke software solutions for financial institutions.
例句：该公司为金融机构提供量身定制的软件解决方案。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Custom-made 定做的
  • Tailored 量身定制的
  • Personalized 个性化的
  • Specially designed 专门设计的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Bespoke" is the past tense of the verb “bespeak” (meaning “to order beforehand”). Originally used for clothing ordered to a customer’s specifications.
bespoke 是动词 bespeak（预定、预约）的过去式，最早用于表示“定制的服装”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: besprecan – to speak or arrange
古英语 besprecan：说出、安排
⬇️
Early Modern English: bespeak – to order in advance
早期现代英语 bespeak：预定
⬇️
Modern English: bespoke – custom-made, tailored
现代英语 bespoke：定制的，量身打造的

📊 Comparison
