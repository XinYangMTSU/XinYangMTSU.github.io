📖 biography /baɪˈɒɡ.rə.fi/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A biography is a written account of someone’s life, usually including detailed facts, events, and achievements.
“biography” 是对某人一生的书面记述，通常包括详细的事实、事件和成就。

Example: She wrote a bestselling biography of Marie Curie.
例句：她写了一本关于居里夫人的畅销传记。
Example: The biography covers his childhood, career, and legacy.
例句：这本传记涵盖了他的童年、生涯与遗产。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Life story 人生故事
  • Memoir 回忆录
  • Profile 人物简介
  • Chronicle 编年史
  • Autobiography 自传

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 "Biography" comes from the Greek roots bios (life) and graphia (writing), meaning “life writing.”
“biography” 源自希腊语词根 bios（生命）和 graphia（书写），意为“记录生命”。

📊 Comparison with Related Words

Word Definition Example Context
Biography A written account of someone’s life The biography highlights her scientific contributions. Books, academic writing
Memoir Personal recollections His memoir focuses on his childhood during wartime. Autobiographical works
Autobiography A biography written by the subject themselves She published her autobiography after retiring. Self-reflection
