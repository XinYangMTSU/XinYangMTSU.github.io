😵‍💫 befuddlement /bɪˈfʌdəlmənt/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A state of being confused or perplexed; mental muddle or bewilderment.
迷惑状态；困惑；混乱。

Example: His explanation only added to my befuddlement.
例句：他的解释只让我更加困惑。
Example: She stared in befuddlement at the strange machine.
例句：她迷惑地盯着那台奇怪的机器看。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Confusion 困惑
  • Bewilderment 迷惑
  • Perplexity 茫然
  • Daze 晕眩；迷茫

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Befuddlement" comes from the verb "befuddle", meaning to confuse or stupefy. It likely derives from “fuddle” (to confuse with drink), plus the prefix “be-”.
“Befuddlement” 源自动词 befuddle，意为使困惑或迷糊，可能来源于“fuddle”（因饮酒而迷糊），加前缀 be-。

🧭 Word Evolution

English: fuddle – to confuse with drink
英语 fuddle：因饮酒而迷糊

English: befuddle – to confuse or perplex
英语 befuddle：使迷惑或困惑

Modern: befuddlement – state of confusion
现代英语 befuddlement：迷惑状态
