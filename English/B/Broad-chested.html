💪 broad-chested /ˌbrɔːdˈtʃɛstɪd/

Part of speech: Adjective
词性：形容词

📖 Meaning:

Broad-chested describes someone (usually a man) who has a wide and strong chest. It often implies physical strength or an imposing appearance.
broad-chested 通常用来形容一个人（多指男性）胸膛宽阔，给人以强壮或魁梧的印象。

Example: The broad-chested athlete stood confidently on the podium.
例句：那位胸膛宽阔的运动员自信地站在领奖台上。
Example: He was tall, broad-chested, and looked like a movie hero.
例句：他个子高大、胸膛宽阔，看起来像电影中的英雄。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Barrel-chested 桶形胸膛的
  • Well-built 体格健壮的
  • Muscular 肌肉发达的
  • Husky 魁梧的
  • Sturdy 结实的

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The term “broad-chested” is a compound adjective from “broad” meaning wide, and “chested,” from “chest,” referring to the upper body area. Together, it literally describes someone with a wide chest.
“broad-chested” 是由“broad”（宽的）和“chested”（胸膛的）组成的复合形容词，字面意思是“胸膛宽阔的”。

🧭 Word Evolution

Old English: brād – wide or broad
古英语 brād：宽的
⬇️
Middle English: chest + -ed → chested
中英语 chest + -ed → chested：带有胸膛的
⬇️
Modern English: broad-chested – wide-chested or strong upper body
现代英语 broad-chested：胸膛宽大、上半身强壮
