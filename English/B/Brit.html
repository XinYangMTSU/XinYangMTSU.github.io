🌟 Brit /brɪt/

Part of speech: Noun (informal)

📖 Definition:

Brit is an informal word for a person from Britain, especially from England.

Example: A clever Brit solved Fermat's Last Theorem after centuries of mystery. (= A British person solved it.)

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • British person
  • Briton (more formal)
  • UK citizen
  • Englishman / Englishwoman
  • Londoner (if from London)

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 Brit is a clipped form of British, used informally.

📝 Its usage became more common in the 20th century, especially in journalism and casual speech.
