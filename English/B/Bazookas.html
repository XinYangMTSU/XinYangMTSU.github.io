💣 bazookas /bəˈzuːkəz/

Part of speech: Noun (plural)
词性：名词（复数）

📖 Meaning:

Plural of “bazooka” – a small, portable rocket launcher used as a weapon, typically fired from the shoulder.
“bazooka”的复数形式；一种小型可携带的火箭发射器，通常从肩部发射，作为武器使用。

Example: The soldiers were armed with rifles and bazookas.
例句：士兵们装备了步枪和火箭筒。
Example: In video games, characters often use bazookas to destroy tanks or walls.
例句：在视频游戏中，角色常用火箭筒摧毁坦克或墙壁。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Rocket launcher 火箭发射器
  • Missile launcher 导弹发射器
  • Anti-tank weapon 反坦克武器
  • Grenade launcher (related) 榴弹发射器（相关）

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 The word “bazooka” was coined during World War II, named after a makeshift musical instrument invented by comedian Bob Burns that resembled the weapon's appearance.
“bazooka” 一词诞生于第二次世界大战期间，来源于喜剧演员鲍勃·伯恩斯发明的一种自制乐器，由于外形相似而被用来命名这种武器。

🧭 Word Evolution

1940s (WWII): Bazooka used by U.S. Army
1940年代（二战期间）：美军使用“bazooka”火箭筒
⬇️
Modern Usage: Refers to military or video game weapon
现代用法：泛指军事或电子游戏中的火箭筒武器
