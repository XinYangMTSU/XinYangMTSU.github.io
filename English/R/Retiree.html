🧓 retiree /rɪˌtaɪəˈriː/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

A person who has stopped working because they have reached a certain age or have chosen to retire.
退休人员；已退休的人。

Example: The park offers discounts for retirees and senior citizens.
例句：该公园为退休人员和老年人提供折扣。
Example: As a retiree, he now spends his time gardening and traveling.
例句：作为一名退休人员，他现在把时间花在园艺和旅行上。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Retired person 退休者
  • Pensioner 领养老金的人（英式英语）
  • Senior citizen 老年人
  • Former employee 前雇员

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Retiree" is formed from the verb "retire" (to leave one’s job) + the suffix “-ee” (one who is the recipient of an action), and came into regular use in the 20th century.
“retiree” 由动词 “retire”（退休）加后缀 “-ee”（表示接受动作的人）构成，在20世纪开始被广泛使用。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin root: retirare – to withdraw
拉丁语 retirare：撤退，退出
⬇️
Modern English: retiree – a person who has retired from work
现代英语 retiree：退休人员
