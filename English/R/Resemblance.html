🧬 resemblance /rɪˈzem.bləns/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

Resemblance means the state of being similar to something or someone, especially in appearance or characteristics.
“resemblance” 意为与某物或某人相似的状态，特别是外貌或特征上的相似。

Example: There is a strong resemblance between the twins.
例句：这对双胞胎非常相像。
Example: His painting bears a close resemblance to Van Gogh’s style.
例句：他的画作与梵高的风格极为相似。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Similarity 相似性
  • Likeness 相像
  • Affinity 相似性；亲和力
  • Parallel 相似之处
  • Correspondence 相符；相似

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Resemblance” comes from the Old French *resembler*, meaning “to seem alike,” which is based on Latin *similis*, meaning “similar.”
“resemblance” 源自古法语 *resembler*，意为“看起来相像”，其根源是拉丁语 *similis*，意思是“相似的”。
