🧱 rigidity /rɪˈdʒɪdəti/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The quality or state of being stiff and not easily bent or changed; structural firmness or resistance to deformation.
刚性；坚硬性；不易弯曲或改变的特性。

Example: The triangle is known for its structural rigidity in architecture.
例句：三角形因其结构上的刚性而被广泛应用于建筑设计中。
Example: Rigidity in thinking can prevent people from accepting new ideas.
例句：思维的僵化可能阻碍人们接受新观点。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Stiffness 僵硬
  • Inflexibility 不灵活
  • Firmness 坚固
  • Resistance 抵抗力

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Rigidity" comes from Latin rigiditas, from rigidus meaning "stiff or unyielding". The suffix -ity forms nouns that express a state or condition.
“Rigidity” 源自拉丁语 rigiditas，rigidus 意为“坚硬的，不屈的”。后缀 -ity 表示某种状态或性质。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: rigidus – stiff, unyielding
拉丁语 rigidus：坚硬的，不能弯曲的

Middle English: rigide – rigid or strict
中古英语 rigide：僵硬的，严格的

Modern English: rigidity – the quality of being stiff or firm
现代英语 rigidity：刚性，僵化

📊 Comparison

Word Formality Level Example Use Tone
Stiffness Neutral The material’s stiffness made it ideal for the bridge. Technical
Inflexibility Semi-formal His inflexibility in negotiations frustrated the team. Critical
Rigidity Formal The design relies on the rigidity of the triangular frame. Objective / Structural
