🤝 reconcile /ˈrek.ən.saɪl/

Part of speech: Verb
词性：动词

📖 Meaning:

Reconcile means to restore friendly relations between people, or to make two ideas, situations, or facts compatible or consistent with each other.
“reconcile” 的意思是恢复友好关系，或使两个想法、情况或事实一致或兼容。

Example: After years of conflict, the two sides finally reconciled.
例句：经过多年的冲突，双方终于和解了。
Example: It's hard to reconcile his words with his actions.
例句：很难将他的话与他的行为协调起来。

🧩 Synonyms

  • Settle 解决
  • Resolve 化解
  • Harmonize 使协调
  • Appease 安抚
  • Mend 修补（关系）

🔍 Etymology of the word

📚 “Reconcile” comes from Latin *reconciliare*, meaning “to bring together again,” from *re-* (again) + *conciliare* (to make friendly).
“reconcile” 源自拉丁语 *reconciliare*，意思是“再次和好”，由 *re-*（再次）和 *conciliare*（使友好）组成。
