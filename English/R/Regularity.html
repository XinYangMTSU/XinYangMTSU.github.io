📏 regularity /ˌreɡjəˈlærəti/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The quality or state of being consistent or occurring at fixed intervals; orderliness or uniformity.
规律性；规则性；有规律的状态。

Example: She exercises with great regularity every morning.
例句：她每天早晨都非常规律地锻炼。
Example: The pattern shows a clear regularity in spacing.
例句：这个图案在间距上显示出明显的规律性。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Consistency 一致性
  • Routine 常规
  • Orderliness 有序
  • Uniformity 统一性

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Regularity" comes from the Latin root regula, meaning "rule" or "straight stick", which also gives us the word “regulate.”
regularity 源自拉丁语 regula，意为“规则”或“直尺”，与 regulate（调节）同源。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: regula – rule
拉丁语 regula：规则
⬇️
Old French: regularité – conformity to rule
古法语 regularité：遵守规则
⬇️
Modern English: regularity – consistency, order
现代英语 regularity：规律性、一致性

📊 Comparison

Word Connotation Example Use Tone
Regularity Positive / Neutral There is regularity in the sun’s rising and setting. Scientific / Observational
Consistency Positive Consistency in training leads to improvement. Motivational
Routine Neutral / Slightly Negative He followed a boring daily routine. Descriptive / Informal
