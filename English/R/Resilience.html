💪 resilience /rɪˈzɪl.jəns/

Part of speech: Noun
词性：名词

📖 Meaning:

The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties or adapt well to adversity; the ability of a material or system to return to its original shape after being bent or stretched.
韧性；适应力；快速从困难中恢复的能力；物体受压后恢复原状的能力。

Example: Her resilience after the accident inspired everyone around her.
例句：她在事故后的坚韧精神激励了周围的每一个人。
Example: The foam mattress is known for its resilience and comfort.
例句：这种泡沫床垫以其弹性和舒适性而著称。

🧩 Synonyms /ˈsɪnəˌnɪmz/

  • Strength 坚强
  • Adaptability 适应力
  • Endurance 耐力
  • Toughness 坚韧

🔍 Etymology of the word /ˌɛtəˈmɑːlədʒi/

📚 "Resilience" comes from Latin resilire, meaning “to spring back” or “rebound.” It is formed from re- (back) + salire (to jump).
“Resilience” 来自拉丁语 resilire，意为“反弹、弹回”，由前缀 re-（向后）和 salire（跳跃）组成。

🧭 Word Evolution

Latin: resilire – to rebound
拉丁语 resilire：弹回

17th century: resilientia – elasticity in materials
17世纪：resilientia 用于描述材料的弹性

Modern English: resilience – ability to recover from adversity
现代英语 resilience：从困难中恢复的能力

📊 Comparison

Word Type Meaning Example
Strength Noun Power or physical force She showed incredible strength during recovery.
Resilience Noun Ability to bounce back from hardship Resilience is key to long-term success.
Endurance Noun Ability to last through stress The athlete's endurance was tested during the marathon.
