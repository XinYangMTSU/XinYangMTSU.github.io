🎌 Japanese Lesson 2: About You

❓ What’s Your Name?

おなまえは？ (Onamae wa?) — What's your name?

👧 I am Ada.

わたしはエイダです。(Watashi wa Ada desu.) — I am Ada.

🎂 How Old Are You?

なんさいですか？ (Nansai desu ka?) — How old are you?

6 Years Old

ろくさいです。(Rokusai desu.) — I’m six years old.
