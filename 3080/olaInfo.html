Late Submission Policy

  • ★ Generally each OLA assignment must be turned in to the D2L repository by the end of the due date.
  • ★ Generally each Programming Project assignment must be turned in to the replit epository by the end of the due date.
  • ★ You may turn in the OLA/Programming Project late by 1 day without penalty if you have reasonable reasonse, e.g. you are in an extrem situation (health issue, family emergecies, and so on). (You need to discuss with the instructor about your egilibility before the due date.)
  • ★ Late Submission Policy:

    • 2 day late (-20 points)
    • 3 day late (-30 points)
    • 4 day late (-40 points)
    • 5 day late (-50 points)
    • 6 day late (-70 points)
    • 7 day late (-90 points)
  • ★ Overdue OLA/Programming Project assignments will not be accepted 7 days after the due date.

Help

  • ★ Feel free to ask the tutor questions if you need help on OLA/Programing Project assignment.

Grading

  • ★ All assignments are graded on a 100 points scale.
