♈ ARIES Fun Quiz! 🐏🔥

Word Bank: Aries, arietine, arietation, arietid

1. The zodiac sign for "ram" is __________.
2. Something "ram-like" is described as __________.
3. The act of butting like a ram is called __________.
4. A meteor shower associated with Aries is called the __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Aries" is Latin for "ram". Here are some related words:

Aries - the zodiac sign for the ram.
Arietine - having ram-like qualities.
Arietation - the act of butting like a ram.
Arietid - a meteor shower linked to Aries.

Word Building Diagram

Aries ➡ Aries (Ram)
Aries + -tine ➡ Arietine
Ariet- + -ation ➡ Arietation
Ariet- + -id ➡ Arietid

Breakdown Explanations

  • ♈ Aries: Zodiac sign meaning "ram."
  • 🐏 Arietine: Describes something ram-like.
  • 💥 Arietation: Butting behavior, like a ram.
  • 🌠 Arietid: A meteor shower linked to the Aries constellation.
