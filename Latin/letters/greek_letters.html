🕰️ Greek Alphabet Timeline

📅 Time Period 📝 Event
🏺 c. 800 BCE Ancient Greeks create their alphabet inspired by Phoenician script.
🏛️ c. 500 BCE Classical Greek alphabet becomes standardized (24 letters).
📜 Hellenistic Period Greek spread widely due to Alexander the Great’s empire.
🕊️ c. 330 CE Early Christian texts written in Greek.
🎓 Middle Ages Greek alphabet used in the Byzantine Empire.
🇬🇷 Today Modern Greek still uses these 24 letters!
