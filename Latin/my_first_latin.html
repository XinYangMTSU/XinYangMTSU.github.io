👋 What is Latin?

Latin is an ancient language that Romans spoke a long time ago! Even today, we still use Latin words in science, history, and even space! 🌙✨

🐾 Let's Meet Some Latin Words!

canis = dog 🐶
feles = cat 🐱
equus = horse 🐴
sol = sun ☀️
luna = moon 🌙

🎉 Fun Fact!

The word canis (dog) gave us the English word canine, which we still use today!
