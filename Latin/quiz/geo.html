🌎 GEO Fun Quiz! 🌎

Word Bank: geography, geology, geometry, geothermal

1. __________ is the study of Earth's surface and people.
2. __________ is the study of Earth's rocks and layers.
3. __________ is math about shapes and spaces (originally used to measure land).
4. __________ energy comes from heat inside the Earth.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Geo" is a Greek root that means "earth". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Geography - study of Earth's surface.
Geology - study of Earth's rocks and structure.
Geometry - math dealing with shapes and space.
Geothermal - heat from inside the Earth.

Word Building Diagram

Geo + -graphy ➡ Geography
Geo + -logy ➡ Geology
Geo + -metry ➡ Geometry
Geo + -thermal ➡ Geothermal

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🗺️ Geo + -graphy = Geography: "Graphy" means "writing or study," so "geography" is "study of the earth."
  • 🪨 Geo + -logy = Geology: "Logy" means "study of," so "geology" is "study of earth's rocks."
  • 📐 Geo + -metry = Geometry: "Metry" means "measure," so "geometry" originally meant "measuring the land."
  • 🔥 Geo + -thermal = Geothermal: "Thermal" means "heat," so "geothermal" is "earth's heat."
