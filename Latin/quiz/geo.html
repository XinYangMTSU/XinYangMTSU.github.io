Word Explanations with Audio

"Geo" is a Greek root that means "earth". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Geography - study of Earth's surface.
Geology - study of Earth's rocks and structure.
Geometry - math dealing with shapes and space.
Geothermal - heat from inside the Earth.
