🐱 FELES Fun Quiz! 🐾

Word Bank: feline, felid, feliform, felinity

1. A __________ animal is one that belongs to the cat family.
2. A lion is a type of __________, meaning a member of the cat family.
3. A creature with a cat-like shape is called __________.
4. The gracefulness or characteristics of a cat is called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Feles" is a Latin root that means "cat". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Feline - relating to or resembling a cat.
Felid - any member of the cat family.
Feliform - cat-shaped.
Felinity - cat-like grace or characteristics.

Word Building Diagram

Feles + -ine ➡ Feline
Feles + -id ➡ Felid
Feles + -form ➡ Feliform
Feles + -inity ➡ Felinity

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐾 Feles + -ine = Feline: Means relating to cats.
  • 🦁 Feles + -id = Felid: Scientific term for any member of the cat family.
  • 🐈 Feles + -form = Feliform: "Form" means shape, so "feliform" means cat-shaped.
  • 😺 Feles + -inity = Felinity: Refers to graceful, cat-like movements or qualities.
