🐬 DELPHINUS Fun Quiz! 🌊

Word Bank: delphinus, dolphin, delphinid, Delphinus constellation

1. The Latin word for "dolphin" is __________.
2. The common English word for "delphinus" is __________.
3. A scientific term for the dolphin family is __________.
4. The constellation shaped like a dolphin is called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Delphinus" is Latin for "dolphin". Here are some related words:

Delphinus - Latin for dolphin.
Dolphin - the common English word.
Delphinid - scientific term for the dolphin family.
Delphinus constellation - the star pattern shaped like a dolphin.

Word Building Diagram

Delphinus ➡ Dolphin
Delphinus + -id ➡ Delphinid
Delphinus ➡ Delphinus constellation

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐬 Delphinus: The Latin root for "dolphin."
  • 🌊 Dolphin: Common English word for delphinus.
  • 🔬 Delphinid: A scientific term for the dolphin family.
  • 🌌 Delphinus constellation: A star pattern shaped like a dolphin.
