🐻 URSUS Fun Quiz! 🐾

Word Bank: ursine, ursid, Ursa Major, Ursa Minor

1. The word __________ means "bear-like".
2. A member of the bear family is called an __________.
3. The Great Bear constellation is also called __________.
4. The Little Bear constellation is also called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Ursus" is a Latin root that means "bear". Many English words and star names come from this root! For example:

Ursine - bear-like.
Ursid - a scientific term for a member of the bear family.
Ursa Major - the Great Bear constellation.
Ursa Minor - the Little Bear constellation.

Word Building Diagram

Ursus + -ine ➡ Ursine
Ursus + -id ➡ Ursid
Ursa + Major ➡ Ursa Major
Ursa + Minor ➡ Ursa Minor

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐾 Ursus + -ine = Ursine: Describes something that is bear-like in appearance or behavior.
  • 🐻 Ursus + -id = Ursid: A zoological term for any bear species.
  • 🌌 Ursa Major: A constellation known as the "Great Bear."
  • 🌟 Ursa Minor: A constellation known as the "Little Bear," home to the North Star (Polaris).
