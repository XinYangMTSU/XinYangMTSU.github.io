🌙 LUNA Fun Quiz! 🌙

Word Bank: lunar, lunatic, lunation, interlunar

1. A __________ eclipse happens when the Earth blocks sunlight from the moon.
2. A person who seems wildly crazy might be called a __________.
3. A __________ is a full cycle of the moon's phases.
4. The time when there is no moon in the sky is called the __________ period.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Luna" is a Latin root that means "moon". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Lunar - related to the moon.
Lunatic - a person once believed to be "moonstruck" or crazy.
Lunation - a full cycle of the moon (new moon to new moon).
Interlunar - the time between an old and new moon, when the moon is not visible.

Word Building Diagram

Luna + -ar ➡ Lunar
Luna + -tic ➡ Lunatic
Luna + -tion ➡ Lunation
Inter- + lunar ➡ Interlunar

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🌕 Luna + -ar = Lunar: "Lunar" means "related to the moon."
  • 🌙 Luna + -tic = Lunatic: In old times, people believed the moon could affect someone's mind, so "lunatic" meant "moonstruck" or "crazy."
  • 🌓 Luna + -tion = Lunation: A full cycle of the moon's phases from new moon to new moon.
  • 🌑 Inter + lunar = Interlunar: "Inter-" means "between," so "interlunar" is the period between moon phases when no moon is visible.
