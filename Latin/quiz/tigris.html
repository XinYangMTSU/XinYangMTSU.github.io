🐅 TIGRIS Fun Quiz! 🌿🔥

Word Bank: tigris, tigrine, tigrine-pattern, Tigris River

1. The Latin word for "tiger" is __________.
2. Something "tiger-like" is described as __________.
3. A common term for tiger stripes is __________ pattern.
4. A famous river named after the tiger is the __________ River.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Tigris" is Latin for "tiger". Some related words are:

Tigris - Latin word for tiger.
Tigrine - having tiger-like qualities.
Tigrine-pattern - a pattern like tiger stripes.
Tigris River - a famous river in Mesopotamia.

Word Building Diagram

Tigris ➡ Tigris (tiger)
Tigris + -ine ➡ Tigrine
Tigrine + pattern ➡ Tigrine-pattern
Tigris ➡ Tigris River

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐅 Tigris: Latin word for tiger.
  • 🔥 Tigris + -ine = Tigrine: Means tiger-like.
  • 🌿 Tigrine-pattern: Common term for tiger stripes.
  • 🌊 Tigris River: A famous river named after the tiger.
