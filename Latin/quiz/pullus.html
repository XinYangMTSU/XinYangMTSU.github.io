🐥 PULLUS Fun Quiz! 🌼

Word Bank: pullus, pullet, poultry, pullulation

1. The Latin word for "chick" is __________.
2. A young hen is called a __________.
3. Farm birds like chickens and ducks are called __________.
4. The act of chicks hatching or multiplying is called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Pullus" is Latin for "chick". Here are some related words:

Pullus - Latin for chick or young bird.
Pullet - a young hen.
Poultry - farm birds like chickens or ducks.
Pullulation - rapid multiplication or hatching.

Word Building Diagram

Pullus ➡ Pullet
Pullus ➡ Poultry
Pull- + -ulation ➡ Pullulation

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐥 Pullus: Latin for chick.
  • 🐔 Pullet: A young hen.
  • 🐓 Poultry: Domesticated farm birds.
  • 🥚 Pullulation: The act of chicks hatching or multiplying.
