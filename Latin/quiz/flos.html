Word Explanations with Audio

"Flos" is a Latin root that means "flower". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Floral - having to do with flowers.
Florist - a person who sells flowers.
Flourish - to grow or develop successfully.
Floriculture - the science of growing flowers.
