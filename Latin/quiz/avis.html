🐦 AVIS Fun Quiz! 🐦

Word Bank: aviary, aviation, aviator, avian

1. A large enclosure for birds is called an __________.
2. The science of flying is called __________.
3. A pilot is sometimes called an __________.
4. Something that relates to birds is described as __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Avis" is a Latin root that means "bird". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Aviary - a large place where birds are kept.
Aviation - the art and science of flying.
Aviator - a pilot.
Avian - relating to birds.

Word Building Diagram

Avis + -ary ➡ Aviary
Avis + -ation ➡ Aviation
Avis + -ator ➡ Aviator
Avis + -an ➡ Avian

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐤 Avis + -ary = Aviary: A large space where birds are kept, like a giant birdhouse.
  • ✈️ Avis + -ation = Aviation: "Aviation" refers to the science of flying, originally inspired by birds.
  • 👨‍✈️ Avis + -ator = Aviator: Another word for a pilot, one who "flies like a bird."
  • 🕊️ Avis + -an = Avian: Something that relates to or is characteristic of birds.
