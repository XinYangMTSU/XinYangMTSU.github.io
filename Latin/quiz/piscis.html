Word Explanations with Audio

"Piscis" is a Latin root that means "fish". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Piscine - relating to fish.
Pisces - the zodiac sign represented by fish.
Pescatarian - a person who eats fish but not other meats.
Piscatory - relating to fishing or fishermen.
