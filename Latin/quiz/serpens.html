Word Explanations with Audio

"Serpens" is Latin for "snake". Many related words come from this root:

Serpent - a snake, often used in stories.
Serpentine - snake-like, winding, or curvy.
Serpens - the constellation of the snake.
Serpentarius - Latin for "snake-bearer," linked to the Ophiuchus constellation.
