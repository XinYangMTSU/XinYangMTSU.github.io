🐍 SERPENS Fun Quiz! 🌿

Word Bank: serpent, serpentine, Serpens, serpentarius

1. A fancy word for "snake" is __________.
2. Something that is "snake-like" is __________.
3. The Latin constellation name for "snake" is __________.
4. The "snake-bearer" constellation was once called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Serpens" is Latin for "snake". Many related words come from this root:

Serpent - a snake, often used in stories.
Serpentine - snake-like, winding, or curvy.
Serpens - the constellation of the snake.
Serpentarius - Latin for "snake-bearer," linked to the Ophiuchus constellation.

Word Building Diagram

Serpens ➡ Serpent
Serpent + -ine ➡ Serpentine
Serpens ➡ Serpens (constellation)
Serpent- + arius ➡ Serpentarius

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐍 Serpens ➡ Serpent: A fancy word for "snake."
  • 🌿 Serpent + -ine = Serpentine: Snake-like or winding in shape.
  • 🌌 Serpens: A constellation that represents a snake.
  • 🌠 Serpent + arius = Serpentarius: The ancient name for "the snake-bearer" (Ophiuchus constellation).
