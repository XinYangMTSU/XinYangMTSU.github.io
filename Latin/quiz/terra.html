Word Explanations with Audio

"Terra" is a Latin root that means "earth" or "land". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Terrestrial - related to the earth or land.
Territory - an area of land.
Subterranean - underground, beneath the earth.
Mediterranean - "in the middle of the land" referring to the Mediterranean Sea.
