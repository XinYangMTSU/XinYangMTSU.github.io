Word Bank: terrestrial, territory, subterranean, Mediterranean

1. A __________ animal lives on land, not in water.
2. A country controls a certain __________ or area.
3. A cave is a __________ space, located below the earth.
4. The __________ Sea is between Europe and Africa.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Terra" is a Latin root that means "earth" or "land". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Terrestrial - related to the earth or land.
Territory - an area of land.
Subterranean - underground, beneath the earth.
Mediterranean - "in the middle of the land" referring to the Mediterranean Sea.

Word Building Diagram

Terra + -strial ➡ Terrestrial
Terra + -tory ➡ Territory
Sub- + terra + -nean ➡ Subterranean
Medi- + terra + -nean ➡ Mediterranean

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🌳 Terra + -strial = Terrestrial: "Terrestrial" means "related to the land."
  • 🗺️ Terra + -tory = Territory: "Territory" means "a piece of land."
  • ⛰️ Sub + terra + -nean = Subterranean: "Subterranean" means "under the earth."
  • 🌊 Medi + terra + -nean = Mediterranean: "Mediterranean" means "in the middle of the land," referring to the sea surrounded by land.
