Word Explanations with Audio

"Leo" is a Latin root that means "lion". Many English words and star names come from this root! For example:

Leonine - lion-like.
Leo - the word for "lion" and a famous zodiac sign.
Leo Minor - a smaller lion constellation.
Leontologist - someone who studies lions.
