Word Bank: solar, parasol, solstice, solarium

1. A __________ panel captures energy from the sun.
2. A __________ is a fancy word for a sun umbrella.
3. The summer __________ is the longest day of the year.
4. A __________ is a room with lots of sunlight.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Sol" is a Latin root that means "sun". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Solar - related to the sun.
Parasol - a sun umbrella.
Solstice - when the sun is at its highest or lowest point in the sky.
Solarium - a room designed to receive sunlight.

Word Building Diagram

Sol + -ar ➡ Solar
Sol + -arium ➡ Solarium
Sol + -stice ➡ Solstice
Para- + sol ➡ Parasol

Breakdown Explanations

  • ☀️ Sol + -ar = Solar: "Sol" means sun and "-ar" means "related to." "Solar" means "related to the sun," like "solar energy."
  • 🏠 Sol + -arium = Solarium: "-arium" means "a place for," so "Solarium" is "a place for the sun" or "a sunroom."
  • 🌅 Sol + -stice = Solstice: "-stice" comes from Latin "sistere," meaning "to stand still." The "solstice" is when the sun appears to "stand still" at its highest or lowest point.
  • 🌂 Para + sol = Parasol: "Para-" means "protection from" and "sol" is "sun," so "parasol" is "protection from the sun" (a sun umbrella).
