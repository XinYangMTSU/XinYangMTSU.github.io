Word Explanations with Audio

"Porcus" is a Latin root that means "pig". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Porcine - pig-like in appearance or behavior.
Pork - meat from a pig.
Porker - a pig raised for food.
Porcupine - "thorn pig," a spiny mammal.
