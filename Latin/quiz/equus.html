🐎 EQUUS Fun Quiz! 🐎

Word Bank: equestrian, equine, equitation, equipoise

1. A person skilled in horseback riding is an __________.
2. Anything related to horses is called __________.
3. The art of riding horses is called __________.
4. The balance or equal distribution of weight is called __________.

Word Explanations with Audio

"Equus" is a Latin root that means "horse". Many English words come from this root! For example:

Equestrian - a person skilled in horseback riding.
Equine - relating to horses.
Equitation - the art and practice of horse riding.
Equipoise - a state of balance (rooted in "equi-" meaning equal).

Word Building Diagram

Equus + -trian ➡ Equestrian
Equus + -ine ➡ Equine
Equus + -itation ➡ Equitation
Equi- + -poise ➡ Equipoise

Breakdown Explanations

  • 🐴 Equus + -trian = Equestrian: "Equestrian" means a rider or horseman.
  • 🧲 Equus + -ine = Equine: "Equine" relates to or resembles horses.
  • 🏇 Equus + -itation = Equitation: This is the formal word for horseback riding.
  • ⚖️ Equi + -poise = Equipoise: "Equi-" means equal, and "-poise" means balance.
