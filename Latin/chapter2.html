⚡ Chapter 2: Roman Gods & Heroes 🛡️

Welcome to the world of mighty gods and brave heroes! In Ancient Rome, people believed in many gods and goddesses who controlled the sky, sea, earth, and even love. 💫

Meet Some Roman Gods:

  • ⚡ Jupiter (Latin: Iuppiter) – King of the gods, ruler of the sky!
  • 🌊 Neptune (Latin: Neptunus) – God of the sea!
  • 🔥 Mars – God of war and courage!
  • 💘 Venus – Goddess of love and beauty!
  • 🌾 Ceres – Goddess of farming and food!
  • 🌍 Terra (Latin: Tellus Mater) – Goddess of the Earth and nature!
🏺 Fun Fact: Did you know the planets Jupiter, Mars, Venus, and Neptune are named after these Roman gods?

👑 Roman Heroes:

  • 🗡️ Aeneas – A Trojan hero who traveled to Italy and became the ancestor of the Romans!
  • ⚔️ Romulus – The legendary founder of Rome!
  • 🛡️ Hercules (Latin: Hercules) – A hero famous for his 12 labors and incredible strength!
🐺 Did you know? Romulus and his brother Remus were raised by a she-wolf in Roman mythology! 🐺
