🕰️ Latin Alphabet Timeline

📅 Time Period 📝 Event
🏺 c. 600 BCE Romans adapt Etruscan alphabet to create early Latin letters.
🏛️ c. 300 BCE Latin alphabet becomes standard in Roman Republic.
⚔️ c. 100 BCE - CE Latin alphabet spreads with the Roman Empire.
📜 Middle Ages Additional letters like "J," "U," and "W" added in medieval Latin.
🇬🇧 Today Modern English alphabet is based on Latin letters!
