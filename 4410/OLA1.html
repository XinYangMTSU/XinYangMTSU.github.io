OLA 1: HTML

Due date: 11:59 pm 01/31/2024

Step 1: Create your first webpage in edStem

It should have the following:

1. a title

Such as John Doe's homepage.

2. a paragraph

A paragraph to introduce yourself.

3. a picture

One of your favorite pictures displayed in size 200px*300px(it can be either local or online, do not forget to upload the photo if you choose to display a local photo)

4. a hyperlink

A link to your favorite website.

5. a list

A list to introduce your hobby. Please use another bulletin rather than None.

6. a table

A table that can display your favorite movies. For example:

Movie Title Director Year
The God Father Francis Ford Coppola 1972
Forrest Gump Robert Zemeckis 1994

7. a Bookmark

Bookmarks to "go to the top" of your webpage

8. headings

Use headings to separate each part (2-8)(use different headings to show different importance)

9. formatting

Use the formatting to highlight your importance.

10. Comments

Add necessary comments to your webpage.

Step 2: Submit

Please submit your OLA1 by clicking submit in edStem.
