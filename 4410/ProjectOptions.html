Final Project Options

1. Web-based Lab System

The Web-Based Lab System is an interactive online platform that enables students to take quizzes, receive instant feedback, and track their performance, while teachers can create and manage assessments, analyze student progress, and generate reports. The system automates quiz grading, ensuring accuracy and efficiency while providing a secure and user-friendly experience for both students and educators.

Student Functionality:

  • ★ Take multiple-choice quizzes online.
  • ★ Receive instant grading and feedback on answers.
  • ★ View grades and detailed explanations for each question.

Teacher Functionality:

  • ★ Create, edit, and delete multiple-choice questions.
  • ★ Automatically grade quizzes and generate reports.
  • ★ View student performance analytics with interactive charts and statistics.

Administrator Functionality: (Optional)

  • ★ Manage user accounts (teachers and students).
  • ★ Monitor quiz activity and system usage logs.

Database Features:

  • ★ Secure Authentication & Password Hashing
  • ★ Quiz & Question Management
  • ★ Automated Grading System
  • ★ Performance Tracking & Analytics

2. Web-based Food Order Management System

The Web-Based Food Order Management System is an online platform that allows users to browse food menus, place orders, and track their purchases in real time. The system enhances efficiency by streamlining order processing, payment management, and inventory tracking for restaurants. Customers can conveniently search for food items, customize orders, and complete transactions securely, while restaurant staff can manage menu updates, track orders, and analyze sales trends.

Customer Functionality:

  • ★ Search for food options by category, cuisine, or price.
  • ★ View detailed food descriptions, images, and availability.
  • ★ Add items to the shopping cart and place orders online.
  • ★ Choose pickup or delivery options.
  • ★ View order history and track real-time order status.

Restaurant Staff Functionality:

  • ★ Add, edit, and remove food items from the menu.
  • ★ Manage food inventory and update availability.
  • ★ Process incoming orders and update order status (e.g., Pending, Preparing, Completed).
  • ★ Generate sales reports and track popular food items.

Database Features:

  • ★ Secure login & authentication.
  • ★ Food Menu & Inventory Management
  • ★ Real-Time Order Processing
  • ★ Order Tracking & History

3. Web-based Library Management System

The Web-Based Library Management System is a digital platform that allows users to efficiently search for books, check availability, and reserve copies online. It streamlines the library management process by providing real-time book status updates, user account management, and automated notifications for reservations and due dates.

User Functionality:

  • ★ Search for books by title, author, or ISBN.
  • ★ View availability status (Available, Checked Out, Reserved).
  • ★ Reserve books online if they are available.
  • ★ View borrowed books and due dates.
  • ★ Leave book ratings and reviews.
  • ★ Receive email/SMS notifications for overdue books or available reservations.

Librarian/Admin Functionality:

  • ★ Add, update, and delete book records.
  • ★ Approve or deny book reservations.
  • ★ Generate reports on book circulation (most borrowed books, overdue books).
  • ★ Manage user accounts and borrowing history.

Database Features:

  • ★ Store book details (title, author, category, availability, ISBN).
  • ★ Maintain user profiles (students, faculty, general users).
  • ★ Track borrowing history and due dates.
  • ★ Secure login & authentication.

4. Web-Based Attendance Management System

The Web-Based Attendance Management System is an online platform that allows teachers to efficiently mark student attendance, track participation trends, and generate reports. The system eliminates manual attendance tracking, reducing errors and ensuring accurate records. Students can conveniently view their attendance history, while administrators can monitor overall attendance patterns and generate analytical reports for decision-making.

Teacher Functionality:

  • ★ Mark student attendance online for each class or session.
  • ★ View real-time attendance records for all students.
  • ★ Generate class-wise or student-wise attendance reports.

Student Functionality:

  • ★ View personal attendance history for each subject.
  • ★ Track attendance percentage for academic compliance.

Database Features:

  • ★ Secure login & authentication.
  • ★ Attendance Record Management
  • ★ Student Performance Analytics

5. Web-Based Music Streaming Platform

The Web-Based Music Streaming Platform is an online music hub that allows users to search for songs, stream music, and create personalized playlists. The system provides an engaging experience where users can browse albums and artists, track listening history, and curate custom playlists. Additionally, independent artists can upload their own tracks, making the platform a space for both discovery and content creation. Secure authentication ensures that users can access their playlists and preferences while providing an optional premium subscription model for exclusive features.

User Functionality:

  • ★ Search for songs, albums, and artists by name, genre, or popularity.
  • ★ Stream music online through an integrated player.
  • ★ Create, edit, and delete custom playlists with favorite songs.
  • ★ Track listening history and recently played songs.
  • ★ Like and save songs for quick access.

Artist Functionality:

  • ★ Upload original tracks for public streaming.
  • ★ Manage song metadata (title, album, genre, and description).

Database Features:

  • ★ Secure login & authentication.
  • ★ Song Metadata Management
  • ★ Real-Time Play Count & Analytics
